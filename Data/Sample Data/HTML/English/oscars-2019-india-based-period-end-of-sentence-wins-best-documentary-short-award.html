Breaking taboos, a film based on menstruation has just won an Oscar. Period. End of Sentence, a documentary that tackles the stigma around menstruation in rural India, is available on Netflix. The 26-minute film revolves around a group of women who use a new machine in order to create low-cost sanitary pads so that women in their village can be financial independent and at the same time improve their feminine hygiene. documentary.org The movie was directed by 25-year-old filmmaker Rayka Zehtabchi and produced by Guneet Monga. What’s interesting here is that a woman director has won the award, which is rare.

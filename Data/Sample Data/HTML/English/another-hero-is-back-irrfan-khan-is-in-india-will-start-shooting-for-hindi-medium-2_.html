Where there is hope, there is a way and looks like the prayers of millions of his fans have been answered. After his long battle with cancer, Irrfan Khan is finally back in India. In March, Irrfan announced that he had been diagnosed with a rare form of tumour. After the diagnosis, the actor along with his family had temporarily shifted base to London for his treatment. We will do a film soon. Actually, we were in the process of writing one for him. Probably, if everything goes well, we shall be making the film soon." There were several reports that hinted at the fact that to ensure recovery, Irrfan wouldn't work for at least a year.

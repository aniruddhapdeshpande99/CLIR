Ajay Devgn Makes A Valid Point Around Star Kids, Asks Trolls To Judge Him But Spare His Kids

Authors : Isha Sharma,

Published on : 20/02/2019, 13:13:41

Ajay Devgn and Kajol's first child Nysa Devgn recently got trolled and body-shamed for her airport look. His kids are often targeted by social media trolls. "Judge me, but don't judge my kids. Kajol and I are the actors, judge us...it's because of us, our children are under the spotlight every time,'' the actor said to IANS.

Just like everything else in life, social media too is a coin that has two sides. For celebrities, sometimes both these sides are extremely heartbreaking. From casual trolling, body-shaming to slut-shaming actors for the choice of their clothes, internet sometimes turn into a place that becomes unbearable. Stars have found their own way of dealing with the negativity that exists on the internet. Some prefer to attack them with their silence but a few celebrities choose to retaliate by giving them justifications and explanations. Instagram Talking about online trolling, Bollywood actor Ajay Devgn recently made a valid point on how star kids are subjected to extreme negativity even though they don't deserve it. Devgn was promoting his upcoming film Total Dhamaal when he explained why social media users should not judge his children. "Judge me, but don't judge my kids. Kajol and I are the actors, judge us...it's because of us, our children are under the spotlight every time. "Being judgmental about someone is not a good thing. If I start passing judgement on someone, that person would obviously feel bad...so do my children. Honestly speaking, those people don't really matter. But I feel bad sometimes when my kids go through such harsh trolling." the actor said to IANS. A lot of times, Ajay's daughter Nysa gets trolled and body-shamed for her airport look and this was not the first time she was targeted. When asked about how her daughter deals with such harsh comments, Devgn said, "She used to get upset over trolling earlier, but now she doesn't care about it anymore.' She knows how to deal with it. She has accepted that there will be some people who will keep judging no matter what." On how to tackle trolls, he said, "Avoid it... If you will react, they will react more and this will continue. So better to stop fighting against trollers." On the work front, Ajay Devgn is currently promoting his upcoming film- Indra Kumar's Total Dhamaal. (With IANS inputs)

After The Success Of Manikarnika, Kangana To Focus On Making More Films On Female Warriors!

Authors : Isha Sharma,

Published on : 03/03/2019, 15:33:03

Kangana made an appearance at the India Today Conclave 2019, where she spoke about a series of movies on women warriors and was quoted as saying, "I'm planning a film on women warriors, and taking a trilogy forward. Manikarnika can be the first part and there can be one on Durgawati or Razia Sultan and there are so many of them."

Kangana Ranaut's last film Manikarnika was controversial, yet successful. Despite all the negativity that surrounded the film, the film managed to shatter a lot of records at the box-office. Be it her acting or her direction, she earned a lot of praises for all the hard-work that she put in for this project.

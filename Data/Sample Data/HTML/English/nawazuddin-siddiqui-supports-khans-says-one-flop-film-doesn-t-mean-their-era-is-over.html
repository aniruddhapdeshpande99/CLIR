Content driven films finally got recognition in the industry. The fact that films like Stree, Badhai Ho and Andhadhun made waves across the nation and received applause from critics and fans alike, was totally unexpected. On the other side, Khans were losing their charm. Salman Khan’s Race 3, Shah Rukh Khan’s Zero and Aamir Khan’s Thugs Of Hindostan failed miserably at the box office.

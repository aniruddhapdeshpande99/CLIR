Indian Air Force’s Wing Commander Abhinandan Varthaman is finally back home and he has been given a hero-like welcome, which he truly deserves. People turned up in huge numbers to welcome the Braveheart back in his home country. Everyone had smiles on their faces as he came back! The entire nation rejoiced yesterday after Pakistani Prime Minister Imran Khan announced that they are sending captured pilot Wing Commander Abhinandan Varthaman back to India as a peace justice. Indian Air Force, however, said that it wasn’t a goodwill gesture but the move was in line with the Geneva Conventions.

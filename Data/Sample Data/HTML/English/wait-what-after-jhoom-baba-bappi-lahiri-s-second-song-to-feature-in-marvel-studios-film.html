In 2017, Hollywood star Chris Pratt said "Whoa... wow" after watching a special Hindi promotional clip of his movie Guardians of the Galaxy Vol.2 that featured Bappi Lahiri's foot-tapping song Jhoom jhoom jhoom baba. Well, that was just a beginning. Looks like this was just the starting point of some more collaborations between Bappi Da and Marvel Studios! Well, yes! According to an agency report, the Indian veteran singer-composer says he is in talks with Marvel Studios for one of their upcoming movies. Over the years, some of the Indian elements have managed to find their way in Marvel superhero films. Remember Robert Downey Jr.'s character Tony Stark (aka Iron Man) attending an Indian wedding in Spider-Man: Homecoming or the Indian slum scenes in The Avengers? And now, if things work out, then one of Bappi Lahiri's songs will feature in a Marvel film. Pinterest "I am currently in talks with Marvel Studios. They had used my song Jhoom jhoom jhoom baba.

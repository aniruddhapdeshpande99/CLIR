Hollywood copied Bollywood?! This year's Academy Awards have finally been concluded and people have a lot of things to talk about. From Rami Malek’s win and fall post win, Lady Gaga and Bradley Cooper’s scintillating performance to red carpet looks, a lot went down at Oscars 2019. e! news Amid all this, one person who stood out the most was Billy Porter, the American stage performer brewed up a storm at the red carpet when he strutted around the Dolby Theatre in a tux-gown. Yes, you read that right! The singer looked dapper in a tux but it wasn’t a conventional one in any sense. The tux had a full skirt which had a personality of its own, mind you. agencies Now, once the hoo-haa subsided, many people (Indians, to be specific) started saying the same thing. Billy Porter might have worn the Tux-Gown but it was our homeboy Ranveer Singh who introduced this trend. Yes, it was Singh who started this fad of wearing a skirt under a jacket. It was during the promotions of his period drama Bajirao Mastani in 2015, that he started wearing skirts.

After a year long of waiting, the Bollywood actress is back with a spectacular performance on the silver screen. Twitter Known for playing women oriented role, Manikarnika is no different. Based on the struggles of Rani Jhansi, Kangana has not only played the tough role but also co-directed the film. She has been going all out to promote the film and the songs. At a recent press conference, the movie was compared to Hrithik Roshan's Mohenjo Daro. Infact, one of the dialogue from Manikarnika's trailer is eerily similar to that of Hrithik's film.

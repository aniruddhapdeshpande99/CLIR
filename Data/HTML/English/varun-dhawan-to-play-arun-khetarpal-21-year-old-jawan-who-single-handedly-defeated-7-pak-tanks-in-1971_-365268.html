One of the bravest officers of Indian Army and also the youngest Param Vir Chakra Recipient, Arun Khetarpal’s story will soon be told to the audiences via a biographical movie. The man who is known for his extra-ordinary service is one of the most influential people in the Indian army. The 21-year-old braveheart single-handedly defeated seven Pakistani tanks before losing his life in the Battle of Basantar during the Indo-Pakistan War of 1971.

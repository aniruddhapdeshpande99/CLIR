With releases like Gully Boy and Brahmastra, Alia surely has a line up of some great releases. The trailer of Gully Boy was unveiled yesterday and has received a big thumbs up from the audience and the film fraternity. The trailer launch event was a fun one as Ranveer Singh was in his full-element like always. Apart from their fun banter, the stars were at their candid best and spoke unabashedly. While interacting with the media, Alia expressed how she considers upcoming superstars of Hindi cinema Ranveer Singh and Ranbir Kapoor as superb human beings and outstanding actors. Alia was interacting with the media at the trailer launch of "Gully Boy" along with his co-actor Ranveer Singh, director Zoya Akhtar and producer Farhan Akhtar and Ritesh Sidhwani on Wednesday in Mumbai. While Alia will be seen opposite Ranveer Singh in Gully Boy, she will also be seen with Ranbir Kapoor in Brahmastra. When she was quizzed and asked to comment about what the actual difference between the two actors is, she said, "There are lots of similarities. Both are superb human beings and outstanding actors. Both of them are really special to me and the only difference between them is that with one of them I am doing 'Gully Boy' and with the other, I am doing 'Brahmastra'." Alia plays the role of a medical student in "Gully Boy" which is based on underground rapping and hip-hop scene of India. She is also collaborating with Zoya Akhtar and Excel Entertainment for the first time in a film.

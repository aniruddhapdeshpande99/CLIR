After Vicky Kaushal's Uri, Ishaan Khatter To Star In A Film On Pulwama Attack, Claim Reports

Authors : Isha Sharma,

Published on : 17/03/2019, 13:07:04

As per reports by a trade website, the film will be helmed by Abhishek Kapoor and the plot will mostly revolve around the Pulwama attack and subsequent air strikes by the Indian Air Force in Balakot, POK. The report further claims that it will be produced by Sanjay Leela Bhansali along with Abhishek and his wife Pragya Kapoor in association with T-Series Films and Mahavir Jain.

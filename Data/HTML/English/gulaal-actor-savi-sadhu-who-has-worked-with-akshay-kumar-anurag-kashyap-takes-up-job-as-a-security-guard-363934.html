Many come to Mumbai with suitcase full of dreams but what lies ahead for them totally depends on their destiny, luck and hard-work. What lies ahead for them is a reality that only they know. In a heartbreaking story, it has come to spotlight that actor Savi Sidhu, who has worked in films like Gulaal, Patiala House and Bevakoofiyan is not being able to make ends meet. In a rather shocking Revelation, Film Companion recently shared a 5-minute long video on YouTube, where the actor shares how after not being able to crack more films and roles in Bollywood,he had to give up on his Bollywood dreams to take up a job as a security guard. Today, he does 12 hours shift as a security guard in a housing society. In the video, he starts talking about his initial days.

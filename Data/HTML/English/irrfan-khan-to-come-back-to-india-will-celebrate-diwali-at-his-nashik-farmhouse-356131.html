The world is collectively praying for actor Irrfan Khan's speedy recovery. The actor is currently in the UK for treatment. He was diagnosed with a neuroendocrine tumor. A few weeks ago, there was a report that claimed how Irrfan Khan will fly back to India to start shooting for the sequel of Hindi Medium. Agencies According to fresh reports, Irrfan will come back to India but he won't start shooting. He will come back to celebrate Diwali with his family at his Nashik farmhouse. A source close to Khan was quoted in a Mid-Day report saying, Agencies "Irrfan and his family are planning to celebrate Diwali at their Nashik farmhouse. It will be a 10-day trip after which he will return to the UK for his ongoing treatment. He is yet to be given a clean chit by his doctors on whether he can start full-fledged work. The doctors are hopeful that he can return to the set by March. But he will have to follow the guidelines that his oncologists give him. The biopic will have to be divided into shorter schedules." Apart from Hindi Medium, Irrfan's upcoming projects also include Shoojit Sircar's Uddhan Singh biopic and Vishal Bhardwaj's Sapna Didi.

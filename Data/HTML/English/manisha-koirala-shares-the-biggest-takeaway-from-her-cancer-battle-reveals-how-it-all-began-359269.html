Manisha Koirala Shares The Biggest Takeaway From Her Cancer Battle, Reveals How It All Began Manisha Koirala Shares The Biggest Takeaway From Her Cancer Battle, Reveals How It All Began When the doctor says “you have cancer”, the world immediately turns upside down, and death seems inevitable. But to survive cancer, is like being greeted a new life. It’s the beginning of a new chapter. Life doesn’t remain the same anymore. Surviving the deadly disease changes one’s perspective towards life completely. Bollywood actress Manisha Koirala has battled cancer. She was diagnosed with ovarian cancer in 2012. She fought, and she survived. Revealing how she healed and how that phase was a lesson in self-discovery, Manisha Koirala has penned a book titled Healed: How cancer gave me a new life. In one of the excerpts, she speaks about her tryst with alcoholism and how the world of showbiz took a toll on her. “To take my mind off shoots, to numb myself, I started drinking. If I was on a diet, it would be vodka. I remember my ex-boyfriend once telling me that I had no sense of balance. He said, ‘You are a workaholic. You either work hard or party hard. Where is your sense of balance?’ Of course I was aware that I had a tendency to go overboard. Many people around me had tried to tell me that."

The Big Fat Indian Wedding! Mukesh Ambani's Daughter Isha To Marry Anand Piramal On December 12

Authors : Isha Sharma,

Published on : 31/10/2018, 12:02:15

Mukesh and Nita Ambani offered the first wedding card to Lord Ganesha at the Siddhivinayak Temple in Mumbai, before they made the official announcement of their daughter Isha Ambani with the son of Swati and Ajay Piramal -- Anand Piramal. Another big fat Indian Wedding!

If we were to coin a hashtag for Anand Piramal and Isha Ambani's impending wedding, it surely would be #RichieRichWeddingBonanaza. After a grander than grand engagement ceremony in Lake Como in Italy, the couple is all set to tie the knot in December. Mukesh and Nita Ambani offered the first wedding card to Lord Ganesha at the Siddhivinayak Temple in Mumbai, before they made the official announcement of their daughter Isha Ambani with the son of Swati and Ajay Piramal -- Anand Piramal. Twitter The statement released in the media reads: Twitter "We are delighted to share that the marriage of Isha Ambani and Anand Piramal will take place in the residence of Shri Mukesh Ambani and Smt. Nita Ambani in Mumbai with family members and close friends on 12th December 2018. The ceremonies will be performed in accordance with Indian traditions, customs, and culture. During the weekend prior to the wedding, the Ambani and Piramal families will host their friends and family in Udaipur, where they are partnering with artisans and craftsmen to integrate local culture and traditions into the festivities. Both families seek the blessings and good wishes of everyone for Isha and Anand as they start their journey of togetherness." Considering how extravagant their engagement ceremony in Italy was, we can only think of the celebrations that will take place for their wedding.

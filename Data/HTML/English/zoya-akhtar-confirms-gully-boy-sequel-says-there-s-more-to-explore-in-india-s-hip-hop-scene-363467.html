And if you are someone who was left wanting for more, here is a good news. Zoya Akhtar has confirmed that the sequel to the film is being planned and executed. And another film exploring the theme is being scripted and planned.” When asked about his brother Farhan and whether he will be seen in the Gully Boy sequel, she said, “He just didn’t fit into Gully Boy. And we’re both sorry about it.” She had a legit reason to why Farhan doesn't fit in into the world of Gully Boy. She added, “Also, his image from the Rock On series is affiliated to an entirely different genre of music. It would have been a bit of a culture shock for the audience to suddenly see him swerve from stadium-rock to hip-hop.” Gully Boy did manage to live upto the hype that it created and emerged as a winner at the box-office. In less than a week of its release, Gully Boy had raked in about 80 crore, as claimed by a report in Box Office India.

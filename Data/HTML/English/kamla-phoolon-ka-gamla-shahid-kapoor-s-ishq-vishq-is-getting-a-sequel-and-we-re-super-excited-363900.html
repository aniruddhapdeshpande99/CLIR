In 2003, there came a film that won our hearts for innumerable reasons. Apart from being Shahid Kapoor and Amrita Rao's debut film, it was a breath of fresh air as Bollywood wasn't doing justice to college romance and the whole idea of old school romance until then. Till today the film remains a delightful watch.

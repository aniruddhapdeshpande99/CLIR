She is making heads turn with her appearances on the award shows and chat shows, proving she indeed is the star to watch out for. Janhvi recently appeared on designer Anaita Shroff Adajania show, Voot’s Feet up with the Stars where she was at her candid best. She confessed that actor Pankaj Tripathi thinks she is a creep. During a conversation with host Anaita Shroff Adajania, she said she has great respect for Pankaj. The daughter of late Sridevi narrated an incident from a party where she heard Pankaj drawing similarities between acting and cooking mutton. Janhvi joined the conversation and expressed her views. Instagram “Sir, par aap toh ice cream ho, aap sabko pasand ho (You are like an ice cream. Everyone likes you). Right after I said it, he gave me a look,” Janhvi said. “He probably thinks I am a creep, but yes I feel he is a legendary actor and there is no one like Pankaj sir. I couldn’t hold back and immediately expressed my fondness for him,” she added.

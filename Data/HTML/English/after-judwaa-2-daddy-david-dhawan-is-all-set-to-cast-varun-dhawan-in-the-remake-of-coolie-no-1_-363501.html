It doesn't come as a surprise that the filmmaker has roped in his son Varun Dhawan to reprise the role that was originally played by Govinda. The news was confirmed by Varun Dhawan himself as he spoke to BBC. He added that the remake would be directed by his father, veteran Director David Dhawan, who had directed the original film. The film also starred Karisma Kapoor. When he was quizzed if Alia would be seen reprising Karisma Kapoor's role, Varun Dhawan refuted the rumour. He added, “I can’t do every film with Alia Bhatt...That you will know soon, I don’t want to say it now but Alia and me are not working.

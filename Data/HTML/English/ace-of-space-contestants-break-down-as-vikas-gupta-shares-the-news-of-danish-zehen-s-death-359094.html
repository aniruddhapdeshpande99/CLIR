'Ace Of Space' Contestants Break Down As Vikas Gupta Shares The News Of Danish Zehen's Death 'Ace Of Space' Contestants Break Down As Vikas Gupta Shares The News Of Danish Zehen's Death One of the saddest news that came in December was the unfortunate demise of 'Ace of Space' contestant and popular blogger Danish Zehen. He succumbed to injuries after a car accident near Mumbai on December 20. Reports claim that Danish was driving back home after attending a wedding when he lost control of his car and hit a roadside wall. This news has left a lot of people from the industry absolutely crestfallen. This list also includes the contestants and the host of Ace of Space, host Vikas Gupta. A clip shared by Vikas shows how difficult it was for him to share the news with the contestants.

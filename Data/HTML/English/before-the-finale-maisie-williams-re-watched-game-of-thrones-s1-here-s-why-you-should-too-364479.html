Just a couple of weeks left for Game Of Thrones season 8 to air, and if you have been planning to re-watch all the past seven seasons, it’s time you do that soon. And if not all the seven seasons, at least re-watch the first one, says Maisie Williams AKA Arya Stark. fanpop.com In order to be ready for the Game of Thrones season 8, Maisie Williams says the first season plays a huge role in what happens in the Game of Thrones season 8. In fact, she herself did the same after having read the script of the finale season, she said. mymodernmet.com “After reading the scripts I went back and watched season one again because so much of it refers back to that season. There are so many scenes that will look similar. And also I watched just to remind myself of the arc I've taken already. I wanted Arya to go full circle and try for some kind of normalcy like when she was younger,” she was quoted as saying to Entertainment Weekly. If you remember the first season, it was quite slow and brooding, right? That is perhaps because there was a lot of set-up that will all make sense in Game of Thrones season 8.

Even Thugs Of Hindostan didn’t work, that doesn’t make Aamir Khan or Amitabh Bachchan bad actors, says the ace singer Sonu Nigam who had an unsuccessful career in acting. Talking exclusively with Indiatimes, Sonu Nigam clarifies why he didn’t continue to pursue acting. Sonu Nigam is undoubtedly the most talented and loved singer in the industry. However, he was unsuccessful as an actor. Nigam who has acted in films like Jaani Dushman, Kaash Aap Hamare Hote, and Love In Nepal, defends his flop acting career saying, Twitter "The thing is I never made a film for myself, Himesh Reshammiya made a film for himself, everybody made films for themselves, I didn’t launch myself, people came to me, so they must have seen something in me. They had seen me performing in my music videos, so they found something in me and came to me, but the films didn’t do well, even Thugs Of Hindostan didn’t work, that doesn’t make Aamir Khan or Amitabh Bachchan a bad actor. Twitter Twitter "Films not working is a different thing, I know what I am, public perception doesn’t matter to me at all.

Political drama films are the latest fad in Bollywood. Beginning the year with The Accidental Prime Minister, Thackeray to now Vivek Oberoi starrer PM Narendra Modi, this genre of films are being made in abundance. Needless to say, the reason is the Lok Sabha Elections 2019 that have kick-started in the country. While the first two saw its release, EC yesterday stalled PM Modi till polling doesn't get over. Another such movie that has run into troubled waters is The Tashkent Files. The movie is a thriller being helmed by Vivek Agnihotri. The Tashkent Files revolves around the mysterious death of India's 2nd Prime Minister Shri Lal Bahadur Shastri and attempts to uncover if he had actually died a natural death or as alleged assassination.

Fondly called the Badshah of Bollywood, the King of Romance Shah Rukh Khan is one of the most loved personalities of the nation. He has a massive ardent fan following that can go to any extent to just catch a glimpse of him. Not only in his home country but people abroad go bonkers every time his movies hit the theatres. He is humble, generous and almost always witty. But did you know that your favourite star Shah Rukh Khan feels like a loser sometimes? Well, that’s what he said recently at the launch event of his forthcoming film Zero’s trailer. “I feel like a loser all the time. I met Aamir Khan a few days back and he asked me why I keep thinking like that. After conversing (with him), I realised he thinks in the same way.” Twitter At the same time, the superstar says that is important to have lack of overconfidence. “Over confidence is worse than feeling like a loser. I feel I work harder when I think I am not good enough. I always feel I'm not good enough. I sometimes joke that I don't know how to dance, I've five expressions but I genuinely believe them, that I'm not good at everything, so I've to work harder,” he told PTI.

As an industry, Bollywood is sometimes a funny place. All it takes is just one picture for media to start floating news of tiffs and break ups. While Luka Chuppi turned out to be a big hit, strong rumors suggested that Kriti Sanon and Kartik Aaryan are not even on talking terms. There were rumours that Kriti was upset with Kartik for hogging the limelight and taking sole credit for the success of the film. Talking to a tabloid, Kriti Sanon had stated, Instagram “This business of overlooking the leading lady has been going on for a while and it’s so unfair. I am glad this conversation is finally happening. It’s logical to talk about only the male protagonist when the leading lady doesn’t have a lot to do, but when they are both carrying a film on their shoulders, the credit should be equally shared. Everybody deserves a mention.” Furthermore, the reports suggested that things are bitter between the two actors. But amidst all these reports, Kriti and Kartik's social media activities hint that all's well.

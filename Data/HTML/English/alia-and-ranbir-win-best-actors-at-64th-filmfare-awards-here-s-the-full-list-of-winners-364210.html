It was a big night for actors like Alia Bhatt, Ranveer Singh, Ranbir Kapoor, Vicky Kaushal and Neena Gupta who were the top winners at the 64th Filmfare Awards in Mumbai on Saturday. The films that took away the biggest awards include Raazi, Andhadhun and Badhaai Ho. In the popular category, Alia won the award for the Best Actor (Female) for Raazi and the film also won the Best Film award (Popular). Ranbir Kapoor won Best Actor (Male) in the popular category for Sanju, while Ranveer Singh (Padmaavat) and Ayushmann Khurrana (Andhadhun) won in the Best Actor category (Critics).

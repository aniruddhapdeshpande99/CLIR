Known for his brilliant comic timing and performances in films like Maalamaal Weekly, Bhool Bhulaiya and Hungama, Rajpal Yadav was sentenced to six months in jail for failing to pay the loan of Rs 5 crore he had taken from Delhi-based entrepreneur MG Agarwal in 2010 at the time of his directorial debut Ata Pata Laapata. He was arrested in Nov 2018, and came out of jail in Feb this year. After having served his jail term, he says some people misused his trust. Rajpal Yadav will soon starting working on a film called Time To Dance alongside Sooraj Pancholi and Isabelle Kaif. He says he is healed, happy and excited to go back to a film set. “I will soon start the shooting of the film Time To Dance alongside Sooraj Pancholi and Isabelle Kaif. We shot the film abroad. A small part of it remains. We will finish that. It could have been finished before,” he told IANS.

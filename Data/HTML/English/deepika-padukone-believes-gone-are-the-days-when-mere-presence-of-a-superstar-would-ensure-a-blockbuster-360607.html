While films like Badhaai Ho, Andhadhun, Stree and Raazi among others struck a chord with the audiences and did a great business at the box office, others, that of the Khans including Aamir Khan’s Thugs Of Hindostan, Salman Khan’s Race 3 and Shah Rukh Khan’s Zero failed miserably at the box office. Content is king, it was proven once again. Gone are those days when mere the presence of a Khan in a film would ensure a blockbuster. Ask Deepika Padukone if script is the most important part of a film, and she says everything boils down to a script. Twitter “It's about the film.

After 'Thugs Of Hindostan' Debacle, Aamir Khan Extends A Sincere Apology To All His Fans After 'Thugs Of Hindostan' Debacle, Aamir Khan Extends A Sincere Apology To All His Fans After the release of Thugs Of Hindostan, everyone had a similar observation to make about Aamir Khan. He either makes the best film or the year or the worst. Clearly, mediocrity isn't his strong suit. The magnum opus tanked at the box-office and opened to horrible review online. After three weeks of the Thugs debacle, Aamir Khan chose to talk about it. Taking "full responsibility" for the film's failure, Aamir Khan spoke at an event where he said, Agencies "I would like to say that I take full responsibility for Thugs Of Hindostan not working with the audience. I think we went wrong. I would like to take full responsibility for that. You can be sure we tried our level best." He also admitted that only a very few people liked the film and he said it is a bad feeling to know that the film you made somehow failed to entertain the audience. He added, "Aur jo audience itni expectation se aayi hai meri film dekhne ke liye, I want to apologise to them also. Iss dafa maine unko entertain nahi kar paaya. Koshish humne puri ki thi. Lekin jo log itne expectation se aaye, unhe mazaa nahi aaya. So, I'm feeling very bad that we were not able to entertain them." Aamir Khan's take on the film's failure is proof that he isn't a sore loser.

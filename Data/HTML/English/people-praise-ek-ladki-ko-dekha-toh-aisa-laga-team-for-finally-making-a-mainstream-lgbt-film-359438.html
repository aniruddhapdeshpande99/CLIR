People Praise 'Ek Ladki Ko Dekha Toh Aisa Laga' Team For FINALLY Making A Mainstream LGBT Film

Authors : Isha Sharma,

Published on : 28/12/2018, 17:19:09

As soon as the trailer was out, people started raving the team for touching the topic. The team was applauded and especially Sonam Kapoor, who has become one of the first few actresses who chose to experiment and take up a subject that many mainstream actors would have not taken up.

"True love mein kuch na kuch siyappa toh ho hi jata hai.." Well, we have seen over a million films revolving around the idea of love and how the characters struggle to find their happy ending but this time, there is something different that deserves our attention. We have seen some brave films touching the topic of homosexuality but never in Bollywood did we see a mainstream film doing justice to it. Well, the change we wanted to see is finally here. The trailer of Sonam Kapoor-Anil Kapoor-Rajkummar Rao-Juhi Chawla starrer 'Ek Ladki Ko Dekha Toh Aisa Laha' was out yesterday and people are overwhelmed. In a first, we will see a family drama that will revolve around the topic that many filmmakers are scared of even touching.

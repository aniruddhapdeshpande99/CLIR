After Salman, Newly-Weds Deepika-Ranveer Will Be Next Guests On Kapil Sharma's New Show After Salman, Newly-Weds Deepika-Ranveer Will Be Next Guests On Kapil Sharma's New Show After being Indian television's most popular man, Kapil Sharma went on to become the most controversial one. With back to back controversies, Sharma's career took a dip and he lost out on a lot of projects. But after a long break, Sharma is all sent to bounce back with his a new season of his old popular comedy show, The Kapil Sharma Show. Sharma has already begun shooting for it. After a fun-filled episode with Salman Khan, reports claim that Sharma is in talks with Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone for the second episode. A Bollywood life report quotes a source saying, Colors "The episode is going to be 'Shaadi' special. Newlywed couples are going to be a part of it as well." Contrary to these reports, the buzz around the show also claims that Singh's Simmba co-star Sara Ali Khan, might accompany him. Whether Ranveer's co-star or his better-half will be seen with him remains a big question, he for sure will be seen on the show in episode number 2. The source further reveals that the show will go on air by mid-January, next year. The source adds, "Kapil is keeping a bank of episodes ready before he goes for his wedding."

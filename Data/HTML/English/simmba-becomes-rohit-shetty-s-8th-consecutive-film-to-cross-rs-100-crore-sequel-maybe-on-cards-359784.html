Simmba Becomes Rohit Shetty’s 8th Consecutive Film To Cross Rs 100 Crore, Sequel Maybe On Cards

Authors : Garima Satija,

Published on : 03/01/2019, 16:31:31

Simmba is Rohit Shetty’s eighth consecutive hit film (after Golmaal 3, Singham, Bol Bachchan, Chennai Express, Singham Returns, Dilwale and Golmaal Again) which has crossed Rs 100 crore mark at the box office. And he is the only Bollywood filmmaker who has done achieved this landmark. Simmba has made more than Rs 124 crore in just five days.

Aamir Khan’s track record was broken recently with the failure of Thugs of Hindostan, which not only tanked in India but also in China – a country that has always showered love upon their Uncle Khan. On the other hand, Rohit Shetty who is hailed as the man with hit formula worked his magic with yet another film recently. With his recipe of success, his film Simmba, which stars Ranveer Singh, Sara Ali Khan and Ajay Devgn, has already crossed the Rs 100 crore.

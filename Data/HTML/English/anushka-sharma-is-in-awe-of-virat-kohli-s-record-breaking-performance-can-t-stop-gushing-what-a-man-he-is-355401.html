It was a remarkable day in Visakhapatnam yesterday. After all, Virat Kohli had hit another century at the India Vs West Indies ODI match. With that, he has also become the fastest cricketer to have completed 10,000 ODI runs. He is now the fifth batsman to score 10,000 runs in ODIs after Sachin Tendulkar, Sourav Ganguly, Rahul Dravid and MS Dhoni. PTI Every cricket fan in the nation went crazy yesterday over his performance. While fans of Sachin Tendulkar and Virat Kohli might still argue who is better, the Master Blaster couldn’t hold himself back from showering praises upon him.

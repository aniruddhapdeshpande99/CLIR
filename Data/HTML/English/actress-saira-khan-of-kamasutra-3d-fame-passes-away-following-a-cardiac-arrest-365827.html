Actress Saira Khan who played the lead role in Kamasutra 3D is no more. The actress who shot to fame after having replaced Sherlyn Chopra in the controversial 2013 film died following a cardiac arrest on Friday morning, according to reports. cineeye.xyz Rupesh Paul, who directed her in the movie, feels she was a great artiste who needed more recognition than she got. Surrounded in grief, Rupesh said that he was even more upset to know that the news of her passing away went unnoticed in the industry. And so he decided to release a statement in the same regard. townnews.com “I found out about the news and was shocked. Later, I noticed that nobody had reported it and that was even more shocking. She deserved to be recognised for her performance and to remain unknown even after the brilliance she had is even more hurtful. It’s a mourning time for us and I hope she Rests in Peace,” he said in a statement as quoted by DNA.

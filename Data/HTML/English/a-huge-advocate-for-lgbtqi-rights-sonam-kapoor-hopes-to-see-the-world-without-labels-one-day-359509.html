A ‘Huge Advocate’ For LGBTQI Rights, Sonam Kapoor Hopes To See The World Without Labels One Day A ‘Huge Advocate’ For LGBTQI Rights, Sonam Kapoor Hopes To See The World Without Labels One Day Breaking stereotypes, Sonam Kapoor is playing the role of a lesbian character in her next film Ek Ladki Ko Dekha Toh Aisa Laga. With the entire nation celebrating decriminalisation of homosexuality, the movie is perfectly timed. A strong supporter of LGBTQI rights, Sonam Kapoor says she is a strong supporter of basic human right – the right to be themselves. Actress Sonam K Ahuja says she is a "huge advocate" for LGBTQI rights, and hopes to see a world without labels one day. "I'm a huge advocate for LGBTQI (Lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer and intersex). For me, it is one of the most important things that I fight for and I hope for when there is a country and a world without labels," Sonam said in a statement. Youtube On the work front, Sonam will also be bringing the complexities of being a homosexual in India with her film "Ek Ladki Ko Dekha Toh Aisa Laga".

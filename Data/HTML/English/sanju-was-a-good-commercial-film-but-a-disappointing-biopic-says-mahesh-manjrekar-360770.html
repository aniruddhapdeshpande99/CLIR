Sanju Was A Good Commercial Film But A Disappointing Biopic, Says Mahesh Manjrekar

Authors : Himesh Mankad,

Published on : 21/01/2019, 12:50:29

Mahesh and Sanjay Dutt have worked together on films like Vastav, Kurukshetra, Hathyar and Viruddh to name a few. And back in the 2000’s, they were said to be the closest pals in the B-Town.“Hirani was on a journey to make a commercial film, and the numbers suggest that he has succeeded in doing so. As a film, I enjoyed it, but as a biopic I was a little disappointed,” he pointed out.

Mahesh Manjrekar, whose Marathi film, Bhai: Vyakti Ki Valli released recently, in an interview with Mirror revealed that he would have directed Sanjay Dutt’s biopic Sanju differently. VVC Films “I have seen Sanju, and it is a well-made commercial film. However, it left a lot unexplored. I would have directed it with a different approach,” said the director, who also made a special appearance in the biopic directed by Rajkumar Hirani. As a film, I enjoyed it, but as a biopic I was a little disappointed,” he pointed out.

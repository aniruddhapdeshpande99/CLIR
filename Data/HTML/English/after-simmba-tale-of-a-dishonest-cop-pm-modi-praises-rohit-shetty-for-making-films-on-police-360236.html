After Simmba, Tale Of A Dishonest Cop, PM Modi 'Praises' Rohit Shetty For Making Films On Police

Authors : Garima Satija,

Published on : 11/01/2019, 15:02:19

When Rohit Shetty met with Prime Minister Narendra Modi recently – along with other young icons of the Hindi film industry that included Alia Bhatt, Ranveer Singh, Vicky Kaushal, Karan Johar and Ashwiny Iyer Tiwari among others – to discuss how the entertainment industry can contribute to nation building, he was praised by the PM for making films on the police force.

If there’s one filmmaker who has cracked the recipe for success, then it’s none other than Rohit Shetty. For the uninitiated, Simmba was his eight consecutive film that crossed Rs 100 crore mark at the box office. Why wouldn’t he be hailed as the man with hit formula, or the undisputed king of the 100-crore club? And if you have noticed, the supporting cast of his films is all the same – mostly actors hailing from the Marathi film industry. Looks like Rohit Shetty is developing his own Avengers (or Khaki) army.

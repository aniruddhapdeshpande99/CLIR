Ranveer and Alia will also bring their Gully Boy energy on the sets of The Kapil Sharma Show, and we’re sure the episode is going to be fun. Did you know that Ranveer Singh wanted to be rapper when he was a kid? On the show, he shared he was always inclined towards rapping. He said he was grateful that Zoya Akhtar decided to make this film, and he had an opportunity to star in it and rap as well. "I think I was in Class 3 or 4 when I first got into hip-hop. I loved listening to Snoop Dogg, Tupac Shakur, The Notorious B.I.G, while growing up and I have been into that kind of music ever since. I used to watch a lot of MTV and that's where I first experienced it. There is a cousin of mine who migrated to the US and whenever he used to come down for summer holidays, he used to bring this music and we used to sit and hear it all the time," Ranveer had earlier told IANS.

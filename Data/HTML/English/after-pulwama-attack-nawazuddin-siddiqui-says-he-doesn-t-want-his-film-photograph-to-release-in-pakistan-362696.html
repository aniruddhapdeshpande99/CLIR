After Pulwama Attack, Nawazuddin Siddiqui Says He Doesn’t Want His Film 'Photograph' To Release In Pakistan

Authors : Garima Satija,

Published on : 24/02/2019, 14:27:09

Now, Nawazuddin Siddiqui, whose film Photograph is making all the waves abroad after multiple screenings, has also stated that he doesn’t want the movie to be released in Pakistan. “I don’t want my film Photograph to release in Pakistan,” said Nawazuddin Siddiqui who has a great fan base in Pakistan.

Despite Average Reviews, Rajinikanth-Akshay Kumar's 2.0 Is Breaking Box-Office Records!

Authors : Isha Sharma,

Published on : 01/12/2018, 15:11:46

2.0 box office day 2: Rajinikanth, Akshay Kumar film earns Rs 38.25 cr in Hindi, is second highest opener ever. In Chennai, the collection has almost doubled on the second day of release. It has grossed Rs 4.77 crore in the city while 2.0 minted Rs 2. 37 crore on day 1.

Rajinikanth and Akshay Kumar's film 2.0 was one of the most anticipated films of the year but did it manage to live up to the hype it created? The film opened to mix-reviews but it has still managed to shatter box-office reports. Agencies In just one day of its release, the film is dominating the box office. Reports claim that the Hindi dubbed version of the movie has earned Rs 38.25 crore after two days. Agencies According to trade analyst Taran Adarsh, 2.0 (Hindi) earned Rs 18 crore on the second day of its release, which was again a working day.

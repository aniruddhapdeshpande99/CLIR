Authors : Isha Sharma,

Published on : 01/01/2019, 10:13:42

Legendary actor Kader Khan, who has made India laugh for years has passed away in Canada. According to reports, the actor was hospitalized in Canada, after he complained of breathlessness. The news of his critical condition got confirmed when Big B extended his prayers for the actor on Twitter.

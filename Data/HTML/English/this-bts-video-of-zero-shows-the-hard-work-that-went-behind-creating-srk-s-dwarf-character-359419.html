However, due to negative word of mouth, the film ended up being one of the mega-disasters of the year. Audience dissed the film and this also led to a dip in the box-office collections. Many failed to notice how a lot of hard-work went behind making this film. A new video shared by Red Chillies gives all of us a glimpse of the hard work that went behind making this dream project a reality. In the video, the VFX producer Ketan Saini says, “We realized that this film couldn’t be shot in a normal way.” And we quite agree. He added that on an average, every shot had to be taken five times and this was possible only because a team of 600 passionate people were shooting for this project. The major challenges involved with the film include creating Lego-like sets and replacing the floor with movable boxes, that were placed so that Shah Rukh’s character could be placed at a lower right.

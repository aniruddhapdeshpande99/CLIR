When it comes to selecting films, script is the most important aspect for Bollywood actor Ayushmann Khurrana. He has given two consecutive hits in 2018 – with Badhaai Ho and Andhadhun. In his next titled Article 15, for which he is collaborating with Mulk fame director Anubhav Sinha, he will be seen playing the role of a cop for the first time ever. Twitter Article 15 of the Indian constitution prohibits decriminalisation on the grounds of caste, religion, race, sex or place of birth. Going by the title, looks like the film will address the address the issue discrimination, just like how Anubhav Sinha’s last film Mulk had. This one, reportedly, is Anubhav Sinha’s most ambitious project until date. As Ayushmann Khurrana wrapped up the shoot of Article 15, he penned down an heartwarming note and called it most “important” and “relevant” film of Indian cinema.

Everyone is aware about the phenomena that Osho is. The spiritual guru Osho aka Acharya Rajneesh has been touted as the most controversial leader of his times. So much so, that streaming giant Netflix made a docu-drama series on him by the name of Wild Wild Country which went onto become a massive hit. While it did show the fandom, Osho had build in Oregon, USA, it shed light of Ma Anand Sheela, his personal assistant and top figure of the Rajnessh cult, who put the entire community together and propelled Osho into a mega brand. After the Emmy winning docu series garnered praises, a lot of filmmakers have approached various actors to adapt this into a film. There are rumours of Alia Bhatt playing Ma Anand Sheela for a Bollywood flick. Priyanka Chopra too has been roped in to play the part by an international filmmaker.

Kareena Kapoor has shattered a lot of stereotypes in Bollywood as a working mother. Even when she was pregnant she walked the ramp with a baby bump making headlines. Soon after giving birth to Taimur Ali Khan, she returned to doing films. But still, she often becomes a target of trolls. pinimg.com Kareena Kapoor had recently given a befitting response to the trolls who slammed her for keeping a nanny for Taimur. “To you judgemental folks who know nothing about my life, please note that there’s a finger I’m showing you in my mind right now,” she had said. But somewhere Kareena feels guilty for leaving her little one at home and going out to work. “I think the most important thing for a working mother is to believe in herself. Even when I leave in the morning, I am constantly worried about what Taimur would be doing at home. There is that looming guilt that is sometimes on your head,” she told Miss Malini. She feels it’s important for a child to see his mother as a working mom. “I think your child will grow up realising, respecting and understanding a woman so much more that when she goes out to work and comes back home, she is still happy.

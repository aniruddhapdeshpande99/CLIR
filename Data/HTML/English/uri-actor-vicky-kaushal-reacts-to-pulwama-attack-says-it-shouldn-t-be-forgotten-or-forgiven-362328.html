Uri Actor Vicky Kaushal Reacts To Pulwama Attack, Says It Shouldn't Be Forgotten Or Forgiven

Published on : 18/02/2019, 11:08:21

"It is a great loss of human lives. If in any possible way, we can support the family of martyrs, it would be a great contribution from our end, as a society. The whole incident should not be forgiven and forgotten," he told the media on Saturday. Read more here.

Reacting to the heartbreaking Pulwama attack that killed over 40 of our soldiers in Pulwama, Bollywood actor Vicky Kaushal described the episode as "sad". Condemning the incident, Kaushal said, RSVP Films "It is a great loss of human lives. If in any possible way, we can support the family of martyrs, it would be a great contribution from our end, as a society. The whole incident should not be forgiven and forgotten." In his latest release Uri: The Surgical Strike, Vicky played the role of an army officer. He received a lot of praise from the audience for his performance in the film.

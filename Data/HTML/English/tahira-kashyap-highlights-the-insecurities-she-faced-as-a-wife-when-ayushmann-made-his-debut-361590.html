Tahira Kashyap Highlights The Insecurities She Faced As A Wife When Ayushmann Made His Debut

Authors : Isha Sharma,

Published on : 05/02/2019, 10:53:07

On the film front, Tahira will start shooting for her first Hindi feature film in March. At the center of the story of this slice-of-life film would be five women who come from different age groups. Cut to 2019. With back to back hits, Khurrana has become one of the most bankable stars. On the other hand, Tahira is battling with cancer and getting all prepped up for he big directorial debut.

For celebrities, talking about the lowest phases of their life requires a lot of courage. Considering their private life is always under the scanner and in public eye, they are scrutinised very closely. However, Tahira Kashyap chose to make an exception. In a recent candid interview, Tahira Kashyap spilled the beans on how her marriage with Ayushmann Khurrana had hit a rough patch during his debut. Khurrana made his debut in 2012 with Vicky Donor and the movie went on to become a blockbuster. However, what we didn't know about that time was how he and wife Tahira were struggling with insecurities and other issues. Tahira confessed that as a wife, she wasn't very accepting about his love-making scenes with Yami Gautam in his debut film Vicky Donor (2012). In a recent interview with Anupama Chopra for Film Companion, author-turned-filmmaker opened up about the rough patch and said, “I was this crazy, insecure pregnant person when Vicky Donor happened and that was the worst phase for the both of us. I think both of us were very immature. I’m sure he didn’t have the maturity to kind of hold my hand and tell me it’s okay, especially, when I am crazy or my hormones because I was pregnant at the time and I also didn’t have the maturity to just take it easy and say that this thing will get over.” She added how insecurities a wife took over her marriage and she was constantly in a zone of self-doubt. She added that it was the foundation of their relationship and marriage that helped them sail through the troubled waters.

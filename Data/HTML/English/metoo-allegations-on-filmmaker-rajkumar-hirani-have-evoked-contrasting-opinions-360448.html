The #MeToo allegations on Rajkumar Hirani have evoked contrasting opinions. A few people are defending the filmmaker and others are still silent. "Raj Kumar Hirani is too good a man to do something like this," said Indian film producer Boney Kapoor while reacting on recent sexual harassment allegations leveled against the Bollywood director. Twitter "I don't believe this allegation. He can never do something like this," Kapoor asserted. Just to join the dots for you, a HuffPost India article reported that a woman (Who was assisting Hirani on Sanju) said that on April 9, 2018, the director first passed a sexually suggestive remark to her and later sexually assaulted her at his home office.

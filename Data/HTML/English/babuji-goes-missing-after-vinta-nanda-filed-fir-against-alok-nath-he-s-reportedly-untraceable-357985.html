Authors : Garima Satija,

Vinta Nanda filed a case against Alok Nath on October 19. Now, according to a report on Mid-day, Alok Nath AKA Babuji is missing. He is untraceable, says a source at Oshiwara police station. Summons were issued to him last week, according to the report, but he was not at home. “We don't know where he is,” added the source.

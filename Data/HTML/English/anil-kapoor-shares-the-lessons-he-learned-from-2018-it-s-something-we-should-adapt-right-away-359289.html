Anil Kapoor turned 62 this year but he looks 26 and there are no conflicts over it. The man believes in making mistakes in life and learning from it every day. As the year is coming to end, we met AK and asked him what he learned from this year. Instagram Anil said, “You learn every day, you make mistakes and then you say let’s not make that mistake and again you make a fresh mistake. Life is a challenge, you face problems every day and you motivate yourself to solve them. This is life, it goes on, but you should just make the most of it”. Now we know how the actor manages to be so jhakaas, his life mantra is just inspiring. This year, we also saw a lot of weddings in Bollywood. Even Anil was super fascinated seeing all the actress turning into gorgeous brides including his daughter Sonam, “It has been a beautiful year for me with all the weddings, I have seen some beautiful brides that’s really lovely. It was so good to see so much ethnicity in the weddings”.

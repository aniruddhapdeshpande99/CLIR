Well, Arshad Warsi believes that the movie would have still been a hit, if the makers had roped him in along with Boman Irani. “I think, let's be honest, if Boman and me were there in Jolly LLB 2, it would've still done a 100 crore. Actually, Fox (producers) would've made more money with Boman and me (laughs),” he was quoted as saying by ZoomTV.

From Tupac To Eminem, Here's Every Rapper That Inspired Ranveer Singh To Prepare For Gully Boy From Tupac To Eminem, Here's Every Rapper That Inspired Ranveer Singh To Prepare For Gully Boy Ranveer Singh's rapper avatar in Gully Boy has got all of us excited. After roles like Khilji and Simmba, the actor is again in a completely different territory, experimenting with films yet again! During a recent interview, the actor confessed his affinity towards the genre since his childhood and his love for Eminem's music. In a statement to IANS, Ranveer said, "Hip-hop is something that's very alive inside me and always has been ever since I was a kid. I have a natural affinity and inclination towards it and I was thrilled to be in Zoya's film because I had always wanted to do such a film." Talking about Gully Boy, he added, "I think I was in Class 3 or 4 when I first got into hip-hop. I loved listening to Snoop Dogg, Tupac Shakur, The Notorious B.I.G, while growing up and I have been into that kind of music ever since. I used to watch a lot of MTV and that's where I first experienced it. There is a cousin of mine who migrated to the US and whenever he used to come down for summer holidays, he used to bring this music and we used to sit and hear it all the time."

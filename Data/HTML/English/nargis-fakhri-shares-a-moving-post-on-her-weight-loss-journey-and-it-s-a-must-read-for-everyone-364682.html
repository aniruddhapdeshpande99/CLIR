After losing 20 kgs in a year, Nargis Fakhri took to her social media account to share the news with a very important message. Fakhri went through a massive body transformation and her post makes a sense because of the nature of the message which is highly empowering. The actor claims to have lost around 20 kgs and shared the news on Facebook with a collage of her ‘then and now’ pictures. She captioned the picture as: “Living life in the public eye can be very difficult sometimes. As much as it is a blessing, it also comes with its downside. Over the last 2 years, I’ve gained weight. On the left, I weighed 178 lbs and on the right, I was 129. I have since lost 20 lbs by making lifestyle changes. If I can do it, so can you. Nurture your mind, body, and soul with positive thoughts and healthy choices. I’m going to bring you on this journey of becoming the best version of myself again, and I want you to join me on this journey too.” For all those who follow her, her body positivity posts are indeed very empowering.

Oscars 2019: Here's The Complete List Of Winners At The 91st Academy Awards Oscars 2019: Here's The Complete List Of Winners At The 91st Academy Awards Oscars are the most coveted award shows across the globe. Whether it's foreign language films, animated cinema or short films, this is one such platform that recognises talent from all corners. And this year was no different. From fresh talent to legendary artists, 2019 Academy Awards had an interesting line up of winners. The show which was held in Dolby Theatre, was a unique one in a lot of ways, This was a first show that had no host. From Queen's electrifying performance to Lady Gaga-Bradley Cooper's Shallow performance, Oscars was mesmerising like always. The list of winners too pleasantly surprised the audience. While Green Book took home the Best Picture award, Rami Malek whisked away the Best Actor award for Bohemian Rhapsody. An India based film Period too made its way into Oscars in the short Documentary movie category and emerged victorious. Here's taking a look at the complete winners list from Oscars 2019.

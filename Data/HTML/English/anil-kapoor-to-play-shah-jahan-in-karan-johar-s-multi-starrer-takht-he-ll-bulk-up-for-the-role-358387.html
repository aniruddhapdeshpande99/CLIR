For the first time, Karan Johar is attempting a historical magnum opus, which his contemporaries including Sanjay Leela Bhansali and Ashutosh Gowarikar have an upper hand in. While not much information has been revealed about Takht as of now, grapevine suggests that Anil Kapoor will play the role of Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in the multi-starrer Dharma Productions film. And to gear up for the role, Anil Kapoor will bulk up and gain weight, it is being said. Twitter “Anil will be putting on a lot of weight to look his part. He is currently extremely lean and fit, but early next year, he will be on a different diet and will gain quite a few kilos before he begins shoot for the period drama,” a source told DNA. And if this was not enough to amp up your excitement level, Karan Johar says the movie will be like his iconic film Kabhi Khushi Kabhi Gham, set in the Mughal era.

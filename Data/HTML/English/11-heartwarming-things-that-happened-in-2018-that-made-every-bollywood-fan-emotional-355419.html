2018 has been a year that has been like a mixed bag for cinema lovers. From fairytale weddings (and wedding announcements) to the explosive #MeToo movement finding its way in India and unmasking several sexual predators, this year has been bitter-sweet. But as we move towards the end of this year, we know a few things: Deepika-Ranveer are together for real, there is no place for sexual predators in the film industry and that Vicky Kaushal is every filmmakers' dream boy now. Too much to digest, isn't it? Pinterest Well to take a look back at this year, we made a list of 11 significant changes that happened this year that left a lot of feelings in our heart. Read on! 1. #EveryDayPhenomenal: Sonam Kapoor and Anand Ahuja's fairytale wedding: After dating for over three years, the couple tied the knot in Mumbai this year. The pictures and videos were all over the internet, making it one of the most popular celebrity weddings of the decade. (And single people like me couldn't sulk more) Vogue India 2. #DeepVeer Wedding: Deepika and Ranveer's wedding confirmation and announcement: The cat is finally out of the bag! Confessing that they were dating for over six years, DeepVeer confirmed that they are indeed taking the plunge this year. Twitter Don't Miss 10.9 K SHARES 3. Badhaai Ho: Content Becomes The King In 2018- Small-budget films winning at the box-office and proving that content in the real king. Films like Parmaanu, Love Sonia, Badhaai Ho, Sonu Ke Titu Ki Sweety and a few others became the real box-office miracles this year. Apart from recovering the cost for the producers, the films managed to earn profits as well. 4. MeToo India: The movement kick-started in India with Tanushree Dutta's claims against Nana Patekar. Thousands of women have come out and shared their similar stories of harassment this year. Calling the predators out, a lot of 'powerful' men of the industry were dismissed and expelled from their respective jobs and projects. 5. Cancer can harm your body, not your spirits: Actors taking cancer in their stride and proving that positivity wins over everything else. People like Irrfan Khan, Sonali Bendre and Ayushmann Khurrana's wife Tahira Kashyap have proved that with a positive mind, it's possible to overcome everything in life. 6. IVF and Surrogacy: Shattering several myths attached to IVF and surrogacy, celebrities including Sunny Leone, Gul Panag, Lisa Ray, and Sakshi Tanwar took the unconventional route and became parents. 7. 'Na Umar Ki Seema Ho': Breaking all stereotypes around age, Milind Soman married the love of his life Ankita Konwar, who is over 15 years younger to him. Instagram 8. When Desi Girl Met Videshi Boy: They came, they met and they fell in love. There was nothing twisted in Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas' love story. The duo met at Met Gala last year but things heated up this year when they met and could never stop. After dating for five months, Nick popped the question and PC couldn't say no! Instagram 9. Hero No: 1: Celebrities joined hands together during Kerala floods for rehabilitation work and proved not all heroes wear capes. Actors like Randeep Hooda, Big B, Sushant Singh Rajput, John Abraham, Arjun Kapoor, Vidya Balan, and a few others came together to help Kerala stand back on its feet. 10. Age no bar: Actors like Manisha Koirala, Surekha Sikri, Neena Gupta, Gajraj Rao became the real stars of the year with some outstanding work. 11. When every Bollywood fan became 'Raazi' For Vicky Kaushal: Vicky Kaushal dethroned everyone else and proved that he is here to stay. With back to back hits like Raazi, Sanju and Manmarziyaan, he became the unprecedented king of box-office.

After making all of us swoon with his stellar performance in the role of Mumbai’s crime lord Ganesh Gaitonde, Nawazuddin Siddiqui is back to enthrall his audiences. And this time, he has donned the role of Shiv Sena’s founder Bal Keshav Thackeray. The trailer of the most awaited film based on the life of Bal Thackeray is finally out. And going by the looks of it, seems like Nawazuddin has aced the role. After watching this goose-bumps inducing trailer, you'll be left wanting for more. Hours before the release, Thackeray was embroiled in a controversy. According to the makers of the movie, CBFC raised objections on four dialogues and two scenes and have asked them to make necessary changes. “Yes, they have raised objections on some dialogues and scenes, four dialogues and two scenes to be precise. We are legally dealing the matter with CBFC and we hope the matter will be sorted out soon," said Shiv Sena Parliamentarian and producer of the film Sanjay Raut.

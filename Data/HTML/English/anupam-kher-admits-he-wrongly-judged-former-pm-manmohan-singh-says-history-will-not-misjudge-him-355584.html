Authors : Garima Satija,

Published on : 28/10/2018, 11:32:26

Anupam Kher is playing the role of Manmohan Singh in his biopic titled, The Accidental Prime Minister, which is based on namesake memoir penned by Singh’s media adviser (2004 to 2008) Sanjay Baru. After having wrapped up the shoot of the movie, Anupam Kher says he was wrong and he “wrongly judged” Manmohan Singh. He added that history will not misjudge him.

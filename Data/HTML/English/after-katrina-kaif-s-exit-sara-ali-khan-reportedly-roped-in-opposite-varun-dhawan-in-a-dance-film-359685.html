Bollywood actress Sara Ali Khan ended the year 2018 with a bang. She made a debut on Koffee With Karan and took everyone by surprise with her graceful answers and poised nature. She won people's hearts during Simmba promotions and proved she's down to earth just like all of us. And then her movies arrived with Kedarnath and Simmba. The actress created fireworks with her performance and the audience has welcomed her into the world of Bollywood. With New Year, Sara Ali Khan has more films in her kitty. As per reports, Sara has been offered a film opposite Varun Dhawan. Yes, she has been considered as the replacement to Katrina Kaif for ABCD.

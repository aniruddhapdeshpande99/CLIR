This is extremely shocking; Superstar Rajinikanth who is in Mumbai to shoot for his next cop drama Darbar along with his leading lady Nayanthara was forced to halt the shoot. Why? As per reports in India Today, the entire crew along with Rajinikanth was shooing in one of the college premises, where the students were strictly asked to not click any pictures or videos of the shoot which left them angry.

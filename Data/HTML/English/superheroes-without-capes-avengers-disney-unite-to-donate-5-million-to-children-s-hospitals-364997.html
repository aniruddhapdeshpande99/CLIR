"The superheroes in 'Avengers' personify traits like courage, perseverance, bravery, and hope — the same traits countless kids and their families in children's hospitals exhibit every day," Robert Iger-the CEO of Disney. Well, in this case-superheroes with capes came in rescue of kids along with Robert Iger, appeared at Disneyland Resort on Friday. The team reunited to announce a $5 million donation to children's hospitals across the country. Agencies The charity is a combined effort of groups including The LEGO Group, Hasbro, Funko and Amazon and the donation of cash and toys will directly be to Starlight Children's Foundation. This comes under the Avengers Universe Unites campaign- which is Walt Disney Company's signature philanthropic commitment to Disney Team of Heroes.

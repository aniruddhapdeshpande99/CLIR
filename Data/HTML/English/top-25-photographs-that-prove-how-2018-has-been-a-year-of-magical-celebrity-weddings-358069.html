How real is the life of reel-life celebrities? As an outsider, we can only imagine and think of how these stars live. How big are their houses? How grand are their cars? While the luxurious lives of these celebrities always appeal to us, commoners, there's much more to these celebrities that we are unaware about. We heavily rely on what we see and how these stars conduct themselves publicly, what we don't see is how real their emotions are! They too fall in love, have their share of heartbreaks, fall in love with their co-stars and have their share of personal and purely private love-stories. 2018 has been a year when many of these love-stories took a beautiful turn in the form of marriage. Three leading ladies of Bollywood and women who have always taken a stand for feminism and equality took the plunge and registered themselves in the married actresses club! In May, Sonam Kapoor got hitched with her longtime boyfriend Anand Ahuja. This was followed by a special winter wedding in November for love-birds Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone and we close this year by a very special cross-culture wedding of Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas.

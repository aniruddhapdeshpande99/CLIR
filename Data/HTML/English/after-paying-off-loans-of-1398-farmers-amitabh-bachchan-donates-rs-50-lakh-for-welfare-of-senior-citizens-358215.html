The legendary star recently donated Rs 50 lakhs for the welfare of senior citizens to a Gurgaon-based NGO, The Earth Saviours Foundation, which is situated in Bandhwari Village. The founder of the NGO, Ravi Kalra, had recently participated on Amitabh Bachchan’s show Kaun Banega Crorepati. Big B was moved after Ravi Kalra narrated the stories of the elderly who are abandoned by theory children. So much so, that he promised to donate, and he indeed did.

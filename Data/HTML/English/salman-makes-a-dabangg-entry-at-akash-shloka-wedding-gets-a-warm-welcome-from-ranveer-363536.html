Akash Ambani and Shloka Mehta's wedding was no less than a star-studded Bollywood Gala. On March 9, several celebrities walked inside the World Centre in Bandra Kurla Complex to congratulate the couple. Present at the wedding were Shah Rukh Khan, Karan Johar, Alia Bhatt, Ranbir Kapoor, Deepika Padukone, Amitabh Bachchan, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Sachin Tendulkar, Yuvraj Singh, Hardik Pandya, Google CEO Sundar Picchai, Uddhav Thackeray, Aaditya Thackeray and several others. All the celebrities turned into Baraatis to welcome the new bride! Salman being Salman arrived late for the ceremonies that evening but something very sweet happened! A video that has resurfaced on the internet has Salman Khan walking in and arriving in his 'Dabangg' style and he is seen being greeted by an excited Ranveer Singh. Twitter Both the stars shared pleasantries and hugged each other! The video is winning the internet and we are not surprised.

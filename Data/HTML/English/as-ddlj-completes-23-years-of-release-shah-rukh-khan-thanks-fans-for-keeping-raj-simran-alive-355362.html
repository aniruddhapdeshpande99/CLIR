Some love stories are classic and timeless and Raj-Simran's love story is one such example. The film managed to win a lot of hearts and it still continues to rule the hearts of many. For many of us, this love story still remains a favorite one. And this year, this love story has completed 23 years of release. On Tuesday night, Shah Rukh took to his social media handles to thank all his fans and followers for keeping Raj and Simran's story alive even after 1200 weeks. He wrote, "A special journey that began 23 years ago, goes on even today. Your love has kept Raj & Simran's story alive on the big screen for 1200 weeks non-stop. Thank u for falling in love with us so unconditionally for so many years! #23YearsOfDDLJ" That's not all, some days back, even Kajol had tweeted, describing similar sentiments.

The third episode of HBO's Game of Thrones was an epic one and for reasons more than one. It was on Sunday that the epic Battle of Winterfell took place and we all know the end! We all saw the epic moment when Arya Stark killed the Night King. People were shocked and surprised to see that Maisie Williams aka Arya Stark saved Winterfell from the dead and managed to stab the supreme leader of the wights with her Valyrian steel dagger. Reacting to this epic scene, Jon Snow actor AKA Kit Harington said that he was both shocked and angry to know that Arya was the chosen one to kill the Night King. Talking to entertainment weekly, Harington said, Twitter “I was surprised, I thought it was gonna be me! But I like it. It gives Arya’s training a purpose to have an end goal. It’s much better how she does it the way she does it. I think it will frustrate some in the audience that Jon’s hunting the Night King and you’re expecting this epic fight and it never happens — that’s kind of Thrones. But it’s the right thing for the characters. There’s also something about it not being the person you expect. The young lady sticks it to the man." For those who haven't seen, in one of the behind-the-scenes video that was released by HBO on Monday, Harington had also jokingly mentioned that he was a little pissed that he didn't get to kill the night king.

Starting her career with the film "Gandhi" in 1982, Neena acted in several films like "Jaane Bhi Do Yaaro", "Khalnayak", "Mandi" among many others. She also hosted the Indian version of the television quiz show The Weakest Link, known as Kamzor Kadii Kaun but she soon fizzled into oblivion because of lack of promising roles.

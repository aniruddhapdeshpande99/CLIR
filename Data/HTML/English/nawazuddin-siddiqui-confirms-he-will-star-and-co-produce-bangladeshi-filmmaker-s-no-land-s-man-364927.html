Nawazuddin Siddiqui Confirms He Will Star And Co-Produce Bangladeshi Filmmaker's 'No Land's Man'

Authors : Isha Sharma,

Published on : 05/04/2019, 13:32:19

The predominantly English-language film will revolve around a tragic, strange, and at times funny journey of a South Asian man whose journey takes layered and complicated shape when he meets an Australian girl in the States, the producers said in a statement.

When it comes to experimentation, no none does it better than Nawazuddin Siddiqui. Be it his unconventional choice of films or his humble nature, he is one of the most loved stars of our country. And now, Nawazuddin Siddiqui is all set to star in Bangladeshi filmmaker Mostofa Sarwar Farooki's No Land's Man, which he will also be co-producing.

During the time of distress, every single act of kindness counts. The film fraternity in India definitely stands in solidarity during toughest of times and with Pulwama, they have again united. After Amitabh Bachchan and Uri Team's generous donations for the martyred soldiers of Pulwama attack, there are other celebrities who are following the footsteps and making contribution in their own way. While Ayushmann's poetic tribute to our soldiers is commendable, Ranveer Singh dedicated his speech at an award function in Dubai to the soldiers and observed a minute of silence. Ranveer Singh on Saturday was felicitated at an award show in Dubai, where he dedicated his speech to the martyred soldiers. During his speech, Ranveer remembered the CRPF soldiers and observe a minute's silence. In his speech, Ranveer said, Bollywood actor @RanveerOfficial urges audience to observe a one minute silence for the martyred Indian soldiers in Kashmir. pic.twitter.com/uKwq6qOpwH — Manjusha (@ManjushaRK) February 16, 2019 "Like every other India, my thoughts and prayers are with our brave jawans who lost their lives in the line of service, our real heroes. May I request everyone present here to join me in observing a minute of silence for our martyred jawans. Please rise from your seats. Let us all please observe a minute of silence for our heroes." 2. Ayushmann Khurrana penned down a heart-warming poem देश का हर जवान बहुत ख़ास है, है लड़ता जब तक श्वास है, परिवारों के सुखों का कारावास है, शहीदों की माओं का अनंत उपवास है, उनके बच्चों को कहते सुना है - पापा अभी भी हमारे पास हैं! -आयुष्मान #Pulwama — Ayushmann Khurrana (@ayushmannk) February 16, 2019 In his poem, Khurrana salutes the courage and sacrifice of soldiers. Salman Khan donates for the families of Pulwama Martyrs through his Being Human foundation The news was shared by Kiren Rijiju, Minister of State for Home Affairs. Thanking Salman and appreciating him for his the contribution, he wrote, “Thank you @BeingSalmanKhan on offering to contribute for Pulwama Martyrs through BEING HUMAN FOUNDATION.

Authors : Isha Sharma,

Kedarnath banned in 7 Uttarakhand districts, disruption of peace expected Sushant Singh Rajput and Sara Ali Khan-starrer, Kedarnath, has been banned in seven Uttarakhand districts by two district magistrates. The state government hasn’t imposed an official ban.

This comes a day after the state High Court dismissed a plea seeking to ban the movie.

Jim Sarbh Feels Disappointed As An Actor, Admits He Receives 'Mediocre' Scripts!

Authors : Isha Sharma,

Published on : 12/04/2019, 12:41:15

Jim Sarbh, who has given critically-acclaimed performances in films such as Neerja and Padmaavat, says he feels underwhelmed with the kind of scripts coming his way. The actor caught the audience's attention as the antagonist in his 2016 debut, Neerja. He has also starred in Sanjay Dutt-starrer Sanju.

In last two years, Jim Sarbh has been one of the biggest revelations as an actor. His critically-acclaimed performances in films such as Neerja and Padmaavat caught everyone's attention. This year, he was seen in Zoya Akhtar's Made In Heaven, that went on to become one of the most discussed web shows. However, as an actor, he admitted that he is disappointed. He admitted that he feels underwhelmed with the kind of scripts coming his way. The actor caught the audience's attention as the antagonist in his 2016 debut, Neerja. He has also starred in Sanjay Dutt-starrer Sanju. Twitter "Films have always been my first love but because I kept getting cast in plays or because the school I went to didn't have film acting course. It just had theatre studies -- I came from a theatrical tradition,'' Jim told PTI. As a performer, Jim added that he is spoilt. He added, Twitter "I have spent the better part of my life reading some of the best plays in the world. I am spoilt. I imagine that each script I get should be as good as those plays and I am often sorely disappointed."

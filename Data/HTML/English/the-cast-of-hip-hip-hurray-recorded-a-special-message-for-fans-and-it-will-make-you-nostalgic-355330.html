The Cast Of Hip Hip Hurray Recorded A Special Message For Fans And It Will Make You Nostalgic The Cast Of Hip Hip Hurray Recorded A Special Message For Fans And It Will Make You Nostalgic For every 90's kid, Hip Hip Hurray was one of the most loved shows. Created and directed by Nupur Asthana, the show completed 20 years on 21 October 2018. To make this 20th anniversary special, the cast of the show including Purab Kohli, Nauheed Cyrusi, Shweta Salve, Vishal Malhotra, Kishwar Merchant decided to take all of us on a sweet trip to nostalgia. To recall the good old days, the cast put out very sweet messages for all their fans. Just to jog your memory a little, the show revolved around the lives of 12th-grade students at the DeNobili High School and how their lives take different twists and turns during their school life. Talking about the show and what it means to him, Purab Kohli said, Screengrab “Hip Hip Hurray changed my life. It placed me in a career and made me do things I never imagined I would be doing.

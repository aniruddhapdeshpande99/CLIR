However, talking about the end of the show, he said, “it’s the most satisfied I will be with my work as Jon Snow.” via GIPHY Game of Thrones is simply one of the most loved shows to grace television screens. For the actor behind Jon Snow - Kit Harington, looking back at the journey of Game of Thrones that brought him name and fame, he revealed the dark time on the show which led him to therapy. via GIPHY Talking about how it was to be playing the role of Jon Snow at the time when the character was at the height of his popularity on the show, Jon Snow said, “My darkest period was when the show seemed to become so much about Jon, when he died and came back.” via GIPHY For Kit Harington, it was the darkest time until the character was resurrected in the next season. “It wasn't a very good time in my life. I felt I had to feel that I was the most fortunate person in the world, when actually, I felt very vulnerable. I had a shaky time in my life around there - like I think a lot of people do in their 20s,” he said. via GIPHY It is that time when Kit Harington had to take help of the therapy. “That was a time when I started therapy, and started talking to people. I had felt very unsafe, and I wasn't talking to anyone. I had to feel very grateful for what I have, but I felt incredibly concerned about whether I could even f***ing act,” he said, via GIPHY He was so helpless that Jon Snow’s fate made it "f***ing terrifying" for him to step out in public. “You get people shouting at you on the street, 'Are you dead?' At the same time you have to have this appearance. All of your neuroses, and I'm as neurotic as any actor, get heightened with that level of focus.” via GIPHY Furthermore, talking about the show and its relevance in today’s world, Kit Harington compared US President Donald Trump to Joffrey Baratheon.

After Padmaavat, we haven’t really seen Deepika Padukone on the big screen, right? After taking a long break owing to her dreamy wedding with Ranveer Singh, Deepika Padukone is all set to be back on the big screen with Chhapaak, a story of a real-life acid survivor. Deepika Padukone will be playing the role of Laxmi Agarwal, who after going through a lot of hardships is now leading her life with courage and bravery. Deepika who earlier said that she was quite moved by the story is happy to be playing this role. Also Read: All You Need To Know About Acid Attack Survivor Lakshmi Who Inspired Deepika's Role In Chhapaak The movie will be directed by Meghna Gulzar, who is known for her works in films like Raazi. For this one, Deepika Padukone has also donned producer’s hat. “You won’t be seeing Deepika, you know. You will be seeing a disfigured Deepika. There is an uncanny similarity between the survivor and Deepika. I am so happy that Deepika is playing the role. I have no right to judge her. How can I judge someone for something which I can’t do? She has played wonderful characters on screen and I believe she will give her best,” Meghna Gulzar was quoted as saying at Lit for Life 2019 event in Chennai.

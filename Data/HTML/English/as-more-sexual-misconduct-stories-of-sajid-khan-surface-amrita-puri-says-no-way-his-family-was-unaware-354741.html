The wave of the movement is spreading across the nation like a wildfire. After Tanushree Dutta accused Nana Patekar of sexual harassment, many big names of the industry have found themselves in trouble. Sajid Khan was directing his forthcoming film Houseful 4, when all hell broke loose for him. After Rachel White, Saloni Chopra and journalist Karishma Upadhyay, more women have accused the filmmaker of sexual harassment. Joining the bandwagon and sharing their win stories are Bipasha Basu, Aisha actress Amrita Puri and ex-Bigg boss contestant Mandana Karimi. Twitter Bipasha Basu also said that Sajid Khan would crack lewd jokes and was rude to all women. “I am glad that women are speaking up about the atrocities of these men with power and clout... But nothing of that sort happened with me... It was just his general attitude towards women that would disturb me on set- he cracked lewd jokes openly and was pretty rude to all girls. I was told by all not to say anything to him and stoop to his level- so I quietly finished my work as a professional and explained my stance to the producers, that I couldn't associate myself with this film anymore as I could lose my temper any moment,” she was quoted as saying by PTI.

Denying Reports Of Stone Pelting During Assam Concert, Singer Shaan Says He Had A Great Time

Authors : Isha Sharma,

Published on : 31/10/2018, 14:12:18

Shaan took to Twitter to clarify that he was not pelted with stones during the concert at Guwahati’s Sarusajai stadium on Sunday as reported. An ANI report claimed that the singer Shaan was pelted with stones and paper balls after he sang a Bengali song during the performance. “Please respect the performing artist, ”Shaan was quoted as saying before walking off the stage after his performance.

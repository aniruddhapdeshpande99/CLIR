No one does promotions better than Ranveer Singh and for Gully Boy, his enthusiasm remains absolutely unmatched. Living up to the expectations of his fans, Ranveer Singh brought to the closing day of a fashion extravaganza here by amalgamating his reel avatar as a rapper from his forthcoming film "Gully Boy" with his real exuberant self. Agencies Ranveer rapped live while walking the ramp with his "Gully Boys" gang to showcase a unique fashion line, GullyGen. Ranveer was present with his team and rapper Naezy and other artistes, sang "Asli hip hop" and "Apna time aayega" from the yet to release film. Agencies The audience obviously was excited and enthralled to watch Ranveer rap live. Talking to a news agency, Singh said, Agencies "Our film is closely connected to the music and the music was the heart of our show. There were unique performances, a live music show... The entire gang has authentic rappers and they contributed to the film, dialogues and music of the film. It was a unique show and a unique experience for me."

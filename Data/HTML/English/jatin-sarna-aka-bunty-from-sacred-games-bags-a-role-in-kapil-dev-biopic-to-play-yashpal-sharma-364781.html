Meanwhile, it has been announced that the man has bagged a role in Ranveer Singh starrer Kapil Dev biopic. In movie titled '83, Jatin Sarna will be seen playing the role of former Indian cricketer Yashpal Sharma. He is elated to have bagged a Kabir Khan directorial. “Well, honestly I am a fan of him since New York and other films which he did. The kind of sensibility and entertainment he serves in his movies grew this desire in me to work with him,” he said in a statement.

When life throws a curve ball at you, you have exactly two options to deal with it. You can either accept your fate and move on positively or cry and rant about how unfair life is. For Sonali Bendre, this curveball came in the form of cancer and as we all know, she chose to deal with it with all the positivity and self-love she had for herself. Currently, the actress is in the U.S, where she is getting treated for metastatic cancer. A lot of her industry friends flew down to visit her but one special person who is lighting up her life these days is Priyanka Chopra. Just a few days ago, Bendre posted on Instagram about her special meeting with Priyanka and her new friend Sophie Turner. She shared the picture with a sweet caption. She wrote, “Was a little low and lonely as @goldiebehl had left for Mumbai… thank you @priyankachopra for lifting my spirits! #AGirlsDayOutIsLikeChickenSoupForTheSoul @sophiet @danasupnick @mimi” But it looks like Sonali is not just fond of Priyanka Chopra but her pet Diana has become her favorite too.

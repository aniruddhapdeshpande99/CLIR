BCCL As sexual harassment allegations against rich and powerful men are pouring out in abundance, a lot of people are facing the wrath of the #MeToo movement. BCCL Talking the same regard, Shatrughan Sinha says he is all for the #MeToo movement but feels that it is now being blown out of proportion.

We all need a circuit in life, don’t we? Munna Bhai MBBS was a game-changer for not only Indian cinema but also for a lot of people involved in the movie – like Sanjay Dutt. The movie revived the career of the star. What’s more? It also marked the directorial debut of one of India’s most sought after directors Rajkumar Hirani. It has almost been 15 years since the first instalment was released, but still, it remains etched in our hearts. So much so, that whenever it airs on TV, its impossible to not watch it. Fans have been waiting for the third instalment for the longest time, and finally, the good news has arrived. The script for Munna Bhai 3 is ready, and Arshad Warsi has confirmed that the shooting will start this year. googleapis.com “I do know that the script is pretty much ready. I do know from Raju [Rajkumar Hirani] that the film will go on floors this year - either mid or the end of the year. The work on the script is going on. As of now, yes it's Sanju and me.

The team has begun shooting the third part in the Dabangg film series in Indore today. In one of the pictures, Salman even teased his fans with a picture of his sunglasses positioned in Chulbul Panday's style. "Day1... Dabangg 3," Salman Khan captioned the post. The team announced the news of the commencement of the shoot by sharing a video of Salman and Arbaaz Khan from Indore. They captioned the post as: "Back in our birthplace for Dabangg 3 shoot." It is reported that Dabangg 3 will be helmed by choreographer and filmmaker Prabhu Dheva. The first installment of the movie series Dabangg, which released in 2010, was directed by Anurag Kashyap while Dabangg " was directed by Arbaaz Khan. Dabangg is Salman's hit franchise that revolves around the life of Chulbul Pandey, a police officer who has his own way of dealing with corruption. The franchise also stars Sonakshi Sinha, Arbaaz and Mahie Gill in key roles.

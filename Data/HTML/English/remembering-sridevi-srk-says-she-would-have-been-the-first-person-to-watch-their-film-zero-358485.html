The actress passed away in February in Dubai. The last film she did a special cameo for was Shah Rukh Khan, Katrina Kaif and Anushka Sharma starrer Zero. The film is heading towards its official release and SRK couldn't stop himself from sorely missing Sridevi. During the promotions of Zero, he recalled his collaboration with Sridevi for his 1996 film Army and how Zero will remain a special film because he again got a chance to share the screen space with her. Khan was quoted in media reports saying, Agencies "Oh, it's a privilege to have had her in the film, because, you know, just the loss is so great. And I hope wherever she is, she makes the world happy like she's done. I kind of started my career when I came and did Army (1996) with her and then she's no more and I have a little scene with her, so it's heartening that way."

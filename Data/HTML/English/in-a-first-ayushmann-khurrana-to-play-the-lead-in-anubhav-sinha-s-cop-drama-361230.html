In A First, Ayushmann Khurrana To Play The Lead In Anubhav Sinha's Cop Drama

Published on : 29/01/2019, 14:59:43

"A chunk of the film will be shot in Kanpur. Like other Ayushmann-starrers this one too is high on content and on a socially-relevant topic. There are some formulas in Bollywood that guarantee you sure-shot success and looks like cop-dramas have turned out to be one of the them. After the success of Simmba, looks like many actors are all set to take the plunge. After the news of Salman Khan collaborating with Rohit Shetty, there is another actor who has signed a cop drama. After back to back hits like Andhadhun and Badhaai Ho, Ayushmann has signed Mulk's director Anubhav Sinha's next. The report states that the film will be set in Uttar Pradesh and will go on the floors in mid-2019. A source has been quoted in a Mumbai Mirror report saying, "A chunk of the film will be shot in Kanpur. Like other Ayushmann-starrers this one too is high on content and on a socially-relevant topic. It is based on a true incident and Ayushmann will play a cop for the first time."

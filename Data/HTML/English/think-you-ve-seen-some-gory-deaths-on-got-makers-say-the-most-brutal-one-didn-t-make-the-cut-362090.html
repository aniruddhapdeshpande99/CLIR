The bloodier the deaths, the better. That’s how the world of Game of Thrones operates. The American fantasy drama series is all about who gets killed and who survives. If you think you have seen the most gruesome deaths on the show, you may be wrong. From Oberyn Martell’s skull being crushed into pieces to Ramsay Snow’s own dogs mauling him to death, from Ser Rodrik Casse being beheaded to Catelyn Stark’s blood oozing out of her throat at the Red Wedding, it’s all about blood and gore on GoT. But do you know that the cruelest death of the show so far didn’t make to the cut? Showrunner David Benioff, in conversation with Entertainment Weekly, revealed that this death of a character was so brutal and tragic that they decided to not showcase it. It was the death of King Tommen's beloved cat, Ser Pounce. Twitter “Cersei hated the name 'Ser Pounce' so much she could not allow him to survive. So she came up with her most diabolical (execution). Ser Pounce's death was so horrible we couldn't even put it on the air,” he said.

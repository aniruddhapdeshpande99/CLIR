On this Reddit thread, people revealed as to why they hate her so much. #1 She is naïve! “She's no where near my least favorite character on the show but I do find myself rolling my eyes at a lot of what she says and does. She's just very naive and entitled and while she fundamentally has good intentions for the people she intends to rule, she has no idea whatsoever on how to get them to actually want her to rule them. She also just sucks at ruling and the only reason she's accomplished anything is because she has dragons, not because she's particularly smart, cunning, or any of the traits the other successful characters of the show have,” wrote a user OddishJihad. via GIPHY #2 She is pretentious and arrogant. “She sits on Dragonstone and when Jon Snow arrives, she immediately asks him to bend the knee. The Starks were the insulted party, after all. She is the one that should have apologized to them in the name of her House,” writes Mindless_Art. via GIPHY #3 She is not the Aragorn - prototype like Jon. “She is a complex character that made a lot of mistakes and she is trying to learn. She is not the Aragorn - prototype like Jon. That's why she is hated,” writes OnlyOneFeeder.

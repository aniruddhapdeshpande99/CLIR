Shahid Kapoor Shares Memory Of His First Meeting With Mira And It Has Full Filmy Feels Shahid Kapoor Shares Memory Of His First Meeting With Mira And It Has Full Filmy Feels Shahid Kapoor and Mira Kapoor might have had started their journey as life partners in an arranged marriage set up but their story has enough 'filmy' twists. Shahid spoke about wife Mira and how parenting works between them on Famously Filmfare and his revelations were interesting. He recalled his first meeting with Mira and the way he explained, it felt like he's reading a scene from a Bollywood movie. He said, Instagram “It was a lovely evening and light was falling on her face. She just turned towards me and smiled and said something. I was like she is so young but I like her, I thought if something is wrong with me." He added that he and his wife Mira have a different approach when it comes to parenting. He says she tells him to calm down a bit when it comes to their two children, while he feels she is “too casual”. I tell her that she is too casual. Having said that, we try and find the middle ground which I think is good for the kids because you need to have both types of parents. He added that when it comes to parenting, balance is an important key. He added, “If both are too obsessive or both are too careless I don’t think that works, so you need to find that balance." On the work front, Shahid is currently shooting for his upcoming film Kabir Singh. (With agency inputs)

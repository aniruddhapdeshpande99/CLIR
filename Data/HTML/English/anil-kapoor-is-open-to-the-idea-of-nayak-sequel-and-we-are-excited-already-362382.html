During a recent interaction with reporters in New Delhi, Anil Kapoor said that the sequel of film Nayak will be a good idea. Starring Anil Kapoor and Rani Mukherji in the lead roles, Nayak was released in 2001. The actor is looking forward for his upcoming release Total Dhamaal.

During a recent media interaction, actor Anil Kapoor expressed his opinions and said that he thinks a sequel to his hit Nayak: The Real Hero will make for a good film. The actor was present on the sidelines of the T20 Dhamaal shoot at a sports channel studio in New Delhi. "I think a sequel to Nayak would be a good idea,'' Anil told IANS when asked which film of his deserves a sequel.

The release date of the biopic is still not finalised but it will release somewhere in 2020. According to a Deccan Chronicle report, Deepika Padukone has been offered to play the role of Ranveer Singh's onscreen wife in Kapil Dev's biopic 83. Helmed by Kabir Khan, the film will majorly revolve around the 1983 World Cup. Whether Deepika has given her nod or not is still a mystery but fans will definitely be delighted if this gets confirmed.

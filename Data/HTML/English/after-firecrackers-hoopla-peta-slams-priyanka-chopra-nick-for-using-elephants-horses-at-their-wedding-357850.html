Authors : Garima Satija,

People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals (PETA) has called out the newlyweds Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas for using elephants and horses at their wedding. PETA India took to Twitter to slam the couple. With that, PETA also shared its video from four years ago, highlighting the woes of the animals, titled, 'Captive Elephants in Jaipur: PETA India's 2014 Inspection'.

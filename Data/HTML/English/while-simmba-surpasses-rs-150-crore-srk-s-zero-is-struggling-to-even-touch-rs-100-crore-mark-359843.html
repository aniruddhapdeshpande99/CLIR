While Simmba Surpasses Rs 150 Crore, SRK’s Zero Is Struggling To Even Touch Rs 100 Crore Mark

Authors : Garima Satija,

Published on : 04/01/2019, 15:01:07

After two weeks of release, Anand L Rai’s Zero has only managed to score about Rs 90 crores at the box office in India. With the release of Rohit Shetty’s Simmba, which has already minted about Rs 150 crores, there seems like no scope of improvement.

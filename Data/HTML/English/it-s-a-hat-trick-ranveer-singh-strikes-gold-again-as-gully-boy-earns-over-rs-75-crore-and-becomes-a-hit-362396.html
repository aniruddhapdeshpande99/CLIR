Authors : Isha Sharma,

Published on : 19/02/2019, 12:45:34

The film that released on 14th February earned an estimated Rs 8-9 crore on its first Monday, which is an achievement for a film as it's a day when footfall in movie theaters are expected to take a dip. According to trade analyst Taran Adarsh, Gully Boy has also surpassed Zoya Akhtar’s earlier hit Dil Dhadakne Do, also starring Ranveer, on Tuesday.

Apna Time Aagaya! Well, Ranveer Singh should scream this loudly as he has again achieved a victorious feet. After two massive hits in 2018 including Padmaavat and Simmba, the actor is ruling the box-office yet again! Helmed by Zoya Akhtar, Gully Boy has lived upto the hype that it created and emerged as a winner at the box-office. In less than a week of its release, Gully Boy has earned about 80 crore, as claimed by a report in Box Office India.

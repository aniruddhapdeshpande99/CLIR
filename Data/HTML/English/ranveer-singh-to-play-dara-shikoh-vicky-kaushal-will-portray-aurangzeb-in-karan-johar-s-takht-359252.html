Karan Johar who is known for making multi-starrer films and for the very first time, he is attempting a historical drama with his upcoming directorial venture. What’s even more exciting about this film is the ensemble cast that includes Ranveer Singh, Kareena Kapoor, Alia Bhatt, Vicky Kaushal, Bhumi Pednekar, Anil Kapoor and Janhvi Kapoor. That’s half of the Bollywood’s most sought-after actors right there. It’s going to be a period drama.

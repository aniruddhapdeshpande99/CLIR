Sonakshi Sinha Reunites With Salman Khan For Dabangg 3, Says It's Like 'Homecoming For Me'

Authors : Saloni Singh,

Published on : 21/01/2019, 10:56:08

Actress Sonakshi Sinha, who will soon start working on 'Dabangg 3', said that shooting for the action-drama film is like homecoming for her as she ventured into Bollywood with the franchise. Sonakshi, who was interacting with the media at Chrome Pictures 15th anniversary celebration and success party of 'Badhaai Ho' on Saturday.

From her fans and her colleagues, they consider this Bollywood actress as the most homely and docile person. And it was purely on the basis of her debut film in 2010. Starring alongside Salman Khan, Sonakshi Sinha in Dabangg created an uproar with her beautiful demeanor. Needless to say, the movie was a massive hit So much so, that Dabangg 2 also came out and was a bigger hit. And now, Sonakshi has confirmed that the action drama is getting a third part. After release of 'Dabangg' and 'Dabangg 2', we took a long break. Now we will begin shooting of 'Dabangg 3'. I have started my film journey with "Dabangg' so it's like homecoming for me so, I am very excited." Sonakshi was seen speaking at the success of Badhaai Ho. She had earlier collaborated with the director of 'Badhaai Ho' Amit Sharma in 'Tevar' , which was his first film as a director. Praising him, Sonakshi said: "I am so proud of Amit Sharma. I am the heroine of his first film so I am so happy for him and so proud of him and the work that he has done is tremendous." Youtube She added that Sharma is one of her 'favourite directors'. Asked is she feels that now small budget films are getting more acceptance from the audience, Sonakshi replied: "I feel it doesn't matter whether your film is a big budget film or a small budget film. If your film's script is good and if it is content driven, then it definitely works at the box office." 'Dabangg 3' is being produced by Arbaaz Khan and directed by Prabhudheva. Inputs from IANS

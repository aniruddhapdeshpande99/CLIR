Big B, Aamir Khan And Ranbir Kapoor Unite To Pay A Musical Tribute To Pulwama Martyrs!

Authors : No Author Specified

Published on : 19/04/2019, 16:18:39

The February 14 terror attack was widely condemned by the Indian film fraternity. Artistes and technicians representing 24 film associations had even held a two-hour protest against the incident, and doubled up as a warning to filmmakers against working with Pakistani talent or releasing their films in Pakistan.

Bollywood superstars Amitabh Bachchan, Aamir Khan and Ranbir Kapoor united and featured in a tribute song dedicated to the 40 slain CRPF troopers who died in a terror attack in Pulwama, Jammu and Kashmir, in February. "Commendable work has been done by Amitabh Bachchan, Aamir Khan and Ranbir Kapoor for the tribute song 'Tu Desh Mera' dedicated to the martyrs of Pulwama. We would like to thank you all for showing your support towards the martyrs," read a post on the official Twitter account of Central Reserve Police Force.

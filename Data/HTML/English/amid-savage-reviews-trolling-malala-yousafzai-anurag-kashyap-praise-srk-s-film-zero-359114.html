Amid Savage Reviews & Trolling, Malala Yousafzai & Anurag Kashyap Praise SRK’s Film 'Zero'

Authors : Garima Satija,

Published on : 23/12/2018, 12:54:06

Highly impressed by Shah Rukh Khan’s performance in the movie, Malala Yousafzai showered praises upon Shah Rukh Khan’s performance in Zero and said that she and her family loved the movie. Anurag Kashyap, too, was highly impressed. Calling Zero a “brave” movie, he said, that the Shah Rukh Khan starrer deserves a viewing for so many grounds it breaks with the mainstream.

If zero fails, maybe I won’t get work for six-10 months, Shah Rukh Khan had said before the release of the movie, clearly hinting that he was skeptical about the success of the film. And why wouldn't he be? Box office numbers and movie business are highly unpredictable. The fact that Shah Rukh Khan’s last release Jab Harry Met Sejal also failed miserably at the box office disappointing his ardent fans, he was eagerly waiting for this one to release and so were his fans. Zero, an Anand L Rai film which also starred Anushka Sharma and Katrina Kaif, opened to mixed reviews with its box office collection dipping low on the very second day. The movie minted Rs 20 crore on day one and Rs 18 crore on day two.

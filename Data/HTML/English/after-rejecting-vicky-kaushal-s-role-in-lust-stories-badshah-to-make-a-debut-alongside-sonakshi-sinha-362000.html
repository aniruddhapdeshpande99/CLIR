After Rejecting Vicky Kaushal's Role In Lust Stories, Badshah To Make A Debut Alongside Sonakshi Sinha

Authors : Garima Satija,

Published on : 12/02/2019, 12:50:59

After revealing about his inclination towards acting on Koffee With Karan, Badshah has finally bagged his acting debut movie. He will be seen alongside Sonakshi Sinha. The movie also stars Kulbhushan Kharbanda, Nadira Babbar Varun Sharma and Annu Kapoor. Producers Bhushan Kumar and Mahaveer Jain have joined hands with director-turned-producer Mrighdeep Singh Lamba for the yet untitled film.

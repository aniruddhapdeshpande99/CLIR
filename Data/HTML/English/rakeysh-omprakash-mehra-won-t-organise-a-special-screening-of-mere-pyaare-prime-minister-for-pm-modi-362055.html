Twitter The trailer shows the story of a young boy named Kanhaiya who lives in a slum where there are no toilets. Just like every other kid, he is bound by his love for mother Sargam. One fine day, when Sargam is on her way back from relieving herself, she is raped. The little boy is, of course, heartbroken. He writes a letter to the prime minister and decides to travel to the national capital with his friends in order to make his voice heard. Watch the deep and intense trailer of Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra’s Mere Pyaare Prime Minister below. The movie is slated to release on March 15. It has become very fashionable, I would rather want to stay away from it.” However, he says, if he wishes to watch it on his own will, it will be a matter of great honour for him. “I feel the film is made for the people of the country and somewhere I don’t want to dilute that by using such a big office. Mere Pyaare Prime Minister stars Anjali Patil, Om Kanujiya and Atul Kulkarni among others.

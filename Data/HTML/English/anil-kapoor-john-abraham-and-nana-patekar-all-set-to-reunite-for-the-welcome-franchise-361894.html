According to a PTI report, John Abraham, Anil Kapoor, Paresh Rawal and Nana Patekar are all set to reunite for the third and fourth installments of "Welcome" franchise. The makers have already started working on the project. Just to jog your memory a little, Welcome was a hit 2007 comedy film directed by Anees Bazmee and produced by Firoz A Nadiadwala. Twitter Starring Akshay Kumar, Katrina Kaif, Feroz Khan, Kapoor, Patekar, Rawal and Mallika Sherawat, the film went on to become a blockbuster. In 2015, the actors reprised their role in Welcome Back and played the roles of underworld dons - Majnu and Uday. The actors will remain same - John, Anil, Nana, Paresh bhai. The third one will be directed by Ahmed Khan (the first two were helmed by Bazmee)," source close to the makers told PTI. The makers added, "The moment 'Welcome 3' will get over in two month's time we will start 'Welcome 4'. It will be the first Indian franchise to have a movies back-to-back.

As 'The Big Bang Theory' Cast Tape The Last Episode, Kunal Nayyar Shares An Emotional Post! 2019 is the year when it all ends. After Avengers and Game of Thrones, another show The Big Bang Theory, which entertained the audiences for 12 years, is coming to an end. The Big Bang Theory cast and crew is in a sad and heartbroken state as they are filming the last 279th episode. The show will always remain close to their hearts, for sure, but now that it is coming to end, it will definitely leave a void in their hearts. The cast members are sharing bittersweet memories of the show, and they are all very emotional. Kunal Nayyar, who played the role of Dr Rajesh Ramayan Koothrappali, shared a photo of the entire cast hugging and penned down an emotional note that read: “Thank you Thank you for your words,Thank you for your encouragement Thank you for tuning in night in and night out Thank you for the stories you shared about how this show made you feel Thank you too, for the not so nice times Thank you, for lifting us up when we were down Fame can feel like a cage, so Thank you for making us feel safe enough to be free. Thank you for letting me share my birthday on stage with my six best friends, And Thank you, for you, because without you- there would be no us. So this goes out to you, the fans... One last time. In love and gratitude, -Kunal” Jim Parsons, who plays the role of Sheldon Cooper shared an emotional note with the picture of apartment 4A, which was Cooper’s residence during all these seasons. Johnny Galecki who plays Leonard shared the picture of Apartment 4B, where he had moved after marrying Penny. “More feelings than words can express,” he wrote. Raunch (Bernadette) shared a cast selfie with the word “forever” and we’re in tears.

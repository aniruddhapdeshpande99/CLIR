The humongous loss of over forty CRPF soldiers who were killed and five others critically injured when a suicide bomber rammed a vehicle laden with explosives into their bus on Thursday is a loss that is going to feel heavy for a long time. The Pakistan-based terror group Jaish-e-Mohammed (JeM) claimed responsibility for the attack on the convoy of 78 vehicles that were on the way from Jammu to Srinagar. In the wake of the attack, Ajay Devgn took to Twitter to announce that his upcoming film ‘Total Dhamaal’ the team has unanimously decided that they will not be releasing the film in Pakistan. Directed by Indra Kumar, ‘Total Dhamaal’, the film is all set to hit the theaters on February 22.

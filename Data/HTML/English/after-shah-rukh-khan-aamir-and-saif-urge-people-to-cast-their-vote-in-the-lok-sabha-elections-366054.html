If you don’t go out and cast the vote, you will lose the right to complain in future. Polling for Lok Sabha elections is already going on, and it’s time to pick your leaders. It’s time to go out and vote. For the longest time we have been complaining sitting behind our computer screens, now is the time to make a difference. And hence, everyone is appealing to the citizens of the nation to vote, including Bollywood celebs like Aamir Khan, Saif Ali Khan and Shah Rukh Khan. Aamir Khan, who is the national icon of the Election Commission Of India, in a video, said, “World's largest democracy, one more time, will choose a new government for itself. Every citizen will get the opportunity to become a part of this process. We often forget that in order to deliver this right to vote to us, our ancestors had sacrificed a lot. So, let's remember them and take an oath that we will definitely cast our vote. For our country, we will fulfil our important responsibility. Jai Hind.” Not voting isn’t an option, said Saif Ali Khan. “Yes... the act of voting in the largest democracy in the world is obviously a very unifying thing. Voting is the most important thing that you can do as a citizen of this country. Young people notoriously do not vote and I think that is the reason we have been brought here today is to speak at this forum to try and influence and point out the importance of that vote."

Shah Rukh Khan Spends Time With Acid Attack Survivors, Writes A Heart-Felt Message For Them

Authors : Garima Satija,

Published on : 29/03/2019, 12:01:33

Shah Rukh Khan who has been supporting the bravehearts of acid attack through his Meer Foundation, recently met with acid attack survivors and spend an afternoon with them. Soon after, he shared pictures on his Twitter handle and wrote a beautiful message along with it. He urged his fans to support his sisters.

Acid attack is even brutal than death because the intent here is not to kill the person but to make him/her suffer for the rest of their life. The survivors of acid attack are brave as well as courageous. However, they do need love and support to be constantly reminded of the fact that beauty isn’t skin-deep.

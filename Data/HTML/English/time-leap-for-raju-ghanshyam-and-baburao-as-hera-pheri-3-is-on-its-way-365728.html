"Yeh Baburao Ka style hai"- As Babu Bhaiya, Paresh Rawal tickled our funny bones several years ago. And now, for all Hera Pheri fans, there is a news that will make you all excited. Thirteen years after Phir Hera Pheri released, the comedy franchise is finally being revived for the third installment. The film will star Akshay Kumar, Suniel Shetty, Paresh Rawal and they will reprise their roles as Raju, Shyam and Baburao Ganpatrao Apte! A Mumbai mirror report has confirmed that the story will take a leap and all the actors will play almost their age in the film. A source was quoted as saying, "It takes off from from where Phir Hera Pheri ended, but fast-forwards into the present." According to reports, producer Firoz Nadiadwala has roped in Priyadarshan for the third installment, who also made the first part of the film.

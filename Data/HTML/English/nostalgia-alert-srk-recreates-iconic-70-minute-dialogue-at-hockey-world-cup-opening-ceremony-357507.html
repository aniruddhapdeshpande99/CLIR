A lot of films on sports have been made in Bollywood, but there still hasn’t been one like Chak De India!. For a country who lives and feeds on cricket, the Shah Rukh Khan starrer was a stark reminder of the feeling of belongingness for national sports in the heart of every Indian. Not only did the movie ignite patriotism, but the compelling plot of the movie gave women sports players an apt depiction on the silver screen. PTI “Sattar(70) Minute, sattar min hai tumhare paas. Shayad yeh.. tumhare zindagi ke sab se khaas sattar minute hai. Aaj tum achha khelo ya bura khelo, yeh sattar minute tumhe zindagi bhar yaad rahenge…” When Shah Rukh Khan had mouthed this very dialogue in the movie, it had sent chills down the spines of every viewer. And yesterday, when Shah Rukh Khan was at the Hockey World Cup 2018’s opening ceremony at the Kalinga Stadium in Bhubaneswar, he charmed the audiences again with his goosebumps-inducing speech.

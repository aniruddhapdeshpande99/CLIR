‘Suits’ Star Patrick J Adams Enjoys Fatherhood, Says His Daughter Taught Him The Value Of Sleep

Authors : Garima Satija,

Published on : 25/10/2018, 18:05:10

Patrick J Adams and his wife welcomed home their little bundle of joy on October 8. As the couple announced the good news to the world, they shared a picture of the newborn girl grasping her parents' hands. It has been just two weeks that he has become a father of his first child with wife Troian Bellisario, and he says, he already has realised how much sleep means.

And to whatever incredible force of fate that brought this baby girl into our lives. I couldn’t be more proud to be her mother. To bring a new girl into this world and to do my best to raise her to be kind, strong and whatever the heck she wants to be.

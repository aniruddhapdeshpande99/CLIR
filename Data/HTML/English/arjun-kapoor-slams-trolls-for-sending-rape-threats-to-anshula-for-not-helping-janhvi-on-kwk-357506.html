For Arjun Kapoor, Anshula Kapoor and Janhvi Kapoor, this situation became real when an episode of Koffee With Karan became a prime reason for trolling and threats. Just to jog your memory a little, in the last episode of Koffee With Karan, Arjun Kapoor made his first public appearance with sister Janhvi Kapoor. In the last segment of the episode, Arjun and Janhvi had to call up a relative and convince them to say "Hey Karan, what’s up”. While Janhvi was the first one out of the two to call up Anshula, she got torn between the two siblings and didn't do what Janhvi told her to do. While all this happened as a fun-light moment on the show, social media trolls took it to a very different level. From fat-shaming Anshula to issuing rape threats, some people became over-aggressive after Janhvi lost the round on the game show to Arjun. Janhvi, at SocialForGood live-athon event, revealed that Anshula received rape threats after the episode. She said, Don't Miss 837 SHARES 3.6 K SHARES instagram "My sister actually just recently got trolled on social media. She did something silly on Koffee with Karan and started getting like rape threats and stuff. And it was just weird to me. As people are faceless on social media, they feel they can have opinions that sometimes cross a moral line." After the situation got intense, Arjun Kapoor too reacted to the whole incident and stood up for her sister Anshula.

Often tagged as 'outsiders', these people enter Mumbai with suitcase full of dreams and begin their journey with the uncertainty of reaching their destination. For Rajkummar Rao, his journey began when he was 16. A Gurgaon guy came to Mumbai with dreams of becoming a dancer. Yes, you read that right. Today, he might be one of the most bankable actors but all he wanted to become back then was a dancer.

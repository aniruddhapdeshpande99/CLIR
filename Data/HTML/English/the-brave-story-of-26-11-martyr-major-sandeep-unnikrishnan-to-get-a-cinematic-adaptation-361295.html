Bollywood has its game totally up for biopics. After back to back hits like Neerja, Bhaag Milkha Bhaag, Dhoni and Sanju, we have yet another appealing story all set for its cinematic adaptation. One of the stories that is all set to be remade into a biopic is of Major Sandeep Unnikrishnan. He lost his life battling the terrorists during the 26/11 attacks on Mumbai in November 2008. Agencies For the uninitiated, Martyr Major Sandeep Unnikrishnan was an Army officer serving with the Special Action Group of the National Security Guards (NSG). Apart from leading a team of NSG commandos to rescue the hostages in Taj Palace Hotel, he was also conferred with the Ashoka Chakra, country’s highest peacetime gallantry award, in 2009. Reports claim that Sony Pictures Entertainment has already registered two titles with the producers’ association called Major and Major Sandeep.

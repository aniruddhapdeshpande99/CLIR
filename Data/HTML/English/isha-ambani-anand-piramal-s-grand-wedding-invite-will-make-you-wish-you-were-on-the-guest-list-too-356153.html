From Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone's wedding to Nick and PC announcing that they will tie the knot, a lot of stories have found their happy ending this year. Another talked-about couple is Isha Ambani and Anand Piramal. Mukesh Ambani and Nita Ambani's daughter Isha is all set to tie to knot with Anand Piramal. The couple got engaged in a grand ceremony in Lake Como, Italy.

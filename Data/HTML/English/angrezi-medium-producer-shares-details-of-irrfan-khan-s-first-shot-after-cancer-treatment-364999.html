Just a few days ago, he penned down a small thank-you note for all his fans and well-wishers who kept in their prayers. As I leave my footprints onto these steps of my life, I want to pause to be grateful for receiving your immense love and support, it soothed me in my process of healing. So I travel back to you, thanking you from the bottom of my heart."

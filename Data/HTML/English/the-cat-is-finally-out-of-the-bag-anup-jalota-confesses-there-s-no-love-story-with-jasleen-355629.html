Anup Jalota Confesses There’s No Love Story With Jasleen From the day Bigg Boss 12 had premiered, Bhajan singer Anup Jalota and his alleged love affair with his co-contestant Jasleen Matharu had set many tongues wagging. After all, a 65-year-old man was in love with a 28-year-old. While some were shocked to hear that, others refused to believe it and called it a publicity stunt. Voot Anup Jalota and Jasleen Matharu entertained the audiences together inside Bigg Boss 12 house for quite some time. However, in the last night’s episode, Anup along with Saba Khan was evicted from the show. But what came as a shocker is that right after his eviction from Bigg Boss 12, Anup Jalota took no time to accept that there was no “love story” between him and Jasleen Matharu. Twitter “People are not able to understand our relationship. We don't have any kind of love story. We just share a musical relationship. She is my student and I am her teacher. I help her in learning music. There is no girlfriend-boyfriend kind of relationship between us and we are also not involved in any romantic and physical relation,” he was quoted a saying by IANS.

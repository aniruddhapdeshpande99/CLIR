Deepika-Ranveer announced on social media that the two are tying the knot and Priyanka threw the most fashionable bridal shower in New York to announce to the world that she's getting married. Priyanka has always broken stereotypes, whether it's her career choices or the man of her life, Nick Jonas. Her fans were rooting that she may get married to an Indian celebrity, but she surprised everyone by taking the plunge with singer-actor of the West - Nick Jonas. As per reports, the duo has finalised the Umaid Bhawan Palace in Jodhpur as their wedding venue. Unlike other celebs who keep their wedding a private affair, PC will be inviting her friends from Bollywood. As per sources of Filmfare, PC has invited Salman Khan along with the other Khan family members. As many of you know, Priyanka was supposed to star opposite Sallu in his upcoming film, Bharat but she walked out of the project last minute citing a clash with her impending wedding. “Besides Salman, invitations will also be sent out to his family members Arpita-Aayush, Arbaaz Khan, Sohail-Seema, Alvira-Atul Agnihotri and Salim Khan. The couple plans to host a 3-day grand celebration at Umaid Bhawan Palace, Jodhpur and 1500-2000 guests are expected to turn up,” a source close to the bride-to-be shared with the publication. Apart from them, Alia Bhatt, Ranbir Kapoor, Katrina Kaif, Farhan Akhtar and Siddharth Roy are also in the list of invitees. We're wondering if King Khan would be called for the royal wedding.

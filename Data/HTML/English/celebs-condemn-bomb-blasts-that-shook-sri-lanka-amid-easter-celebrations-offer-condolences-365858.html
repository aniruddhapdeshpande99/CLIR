The people in Sri Lanka were in a mood to celebrate. Worshippers gathered at Roman Catholic churches across the country to celebrate Easter Mass, the culmination of Holy Week, but a wave of explosion rocked the worshippers. Around 8:45 am, eight bombing attacks at, at least three churches, took the lives of more than 207 people injuring 400 others at churches and hotels, according to BBC. Hotels including Shangri-La, Kingsbury, Cinnamon Grand and another one were also hit. Police have arrested three people so far, according to theguardian.com.

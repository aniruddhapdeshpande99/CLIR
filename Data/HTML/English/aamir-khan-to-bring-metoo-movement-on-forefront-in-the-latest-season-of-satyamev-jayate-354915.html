Aamir Khan is one of those rare actors who managed to strike a positive chord with the audience and his fans through his TV appearance. The last season Aamir Khan's show 'Satyamev Jayate' aired in October 2014 and the next season is now on the cards. Aamir, who is a 'rebel with a cause' makes sure that he picks up topics and issues that are both relevant and important. Star India For the fourth season of the show, Aamir has decided to bring the #MeToo movement on the forefront. Reports also claim that Aamir will be inviting all the #MeToo survivors to share their survival stories. A source has been quoted in the report as saying, Star Plus "With several women opening up about sexual harassment at workplaces and otherwise, Aamir wants to throw light on their problems and the issue of their safety. He has told his team to start doing research on it. He will invite the #MeToo survivors to share their horrifying experiences. He will also get some of the alleged accused on the show to present their point of view." Aamir has been associated with TV show Satyamev Jayate for years and the TRP's of the show prove that his honesty and sincerity on the show is genuinely loved.

Complaint Filed Against Sara Ali Khan For Riding Pillion Without Helmet, Police Say Action Will Be Taken

Authors : Garima Satija,

Published on : 11/04/2019, 12:07:54

Police complaint has been filed against Sara Ali Khan. In a statement, Delhi Police have said that necessary action will be taken against the actress under the Motor Vehicles Act after facts pertaining to the incident are received, perused and verified. In the video, Kartik Aryan can be seen riding the bike. Sara Ali Khan is sitting behind him. Sara Ali Khan is not wearing the helmet.

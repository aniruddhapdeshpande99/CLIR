A perfect girls night out includes copious amount of alcohol, great music and loads of fun and Adele and Jennifer Lawrence did exactly that! For Bar patrons in New York’s Greenwich Village, Friday was a little different from other evenings. They just began partying when Adele and Jennifer Lawrence showed up. According to news reports Grammy-winning singer Adele and her Oscar-winning actor pal hit the gay bar Pieces on Friday night, to the delight of the crowd. They drank and danced and schmoozed, hugging shirtless men and taking selfies while the crowd applauded. They danced to Kylie Minogue and played a drinking game. Adele participated in a game show hosted by a drag queen and introduced herself as a married, stay-at-home mom. More videos from the party show Jennifer sitting in Adele’s lap and getting lesson in drag make up. She can be heard talking about getting the ‘Chinese brows’.

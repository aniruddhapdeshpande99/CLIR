After putting up a brave fight against cancer in London, Irrfan Khan is back home in Mumbai. The actor returned from London last month after undergoing treatment for neuroendocrine tumour. His illness kept him away from his work for about a year but the actor has finally returned to the film sets and it's a sight to behold for all his fans and well-wishers who were praying for his recovery. The actor has started shooting for the sequel to Hindi Medium, titled Angrezi Medium in Udaipur. The team shared the first mahurat pictures from the sets in Udaipur and people are delighted to see Irrfan Khan back in full-form. Twitter Twitter Directed by Homi Adajania, the film will be produced by Dinesh Vijan.

The film fraternity might not have been fair to Kangana Ranaut but she for sure knows the purpose of her existence in the films. It won't be wrong to say that she has repeatedly been snubbed and boycotted by the so-called A-listers of the film fraternity. She took a stand for herself and released her film Manikarnika on her own terms and conditions. Despite the cloud of negativity and controversies hovering around her project, the film managed to claim the status of a blockbuster. (Also read: 7 Controversial Statements By Kangana Ranaut, After The Release Of Manikarnika, That Define Her) Yesterday Ranaut was speaking at the India Today Annual conclave where she was again in her unabashed avatar. She also spoke about her controversial episode with Hrithik Roshan “Talking about this so-called case which never actually happened, here’s a person I have worked with in two films. When she was asked if she would be interested in swapping places with Karan and Hrithik, she said, “He will never be where I am. I come from a situation where I really do not have a backup plan. I don’t have a plan B. It’s not like I wake up in the morning and think about ‘Oh wait a minute. How many people can I offend today and how to make my life difficult?’ That’s not how it is. 5. During her interaction, she also added that feminism is a misused word for a lot of reasons. She said, “Feminism is a word which is misused and especially in today’s time and age. I know so many people who know nothing about equality and they wear these badges of feminism, pay parity and fighting cause...,” she said. “I don’t go by the book definition of feminism. I have made my own definition. Though the word is derived from feminine but why should there be only equalities which is influenced by or related to sex. There should be equality of life... I think with time we need to evolve the definition."

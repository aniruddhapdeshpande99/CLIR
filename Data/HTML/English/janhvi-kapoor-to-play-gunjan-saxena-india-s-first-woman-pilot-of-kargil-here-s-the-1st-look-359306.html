The braveheart, along with her fellow lieutenant Srividya Rajan faced a lot of hurdles, but she emerged a warrior and a fighter. Following the trend of biopics in Bollywood, Karan Johar is making a film on the Gunjan Saxena, and as it was rumoured earlier, Janhvi Kapoor is starring in the titular role. Sridevi’s daughter Janhvi Kapoor had raised many eyebrows and has also kickstarted the nepotism debate yet again after it was announced that she is making her acting debut in a Karan Johar film titled Dhadak. While she is also doing another film with Karan Johar, which is a multi-starrer historical drama Takht, she has another one on cards with KJo – a biopic on Indian Air Force Pilots Gunjan Saxena. Here’s the first look! While earlier rumours were rife that Dulquer Salman will play Janhvi Kapoor’s love interest in the movie, now the grapevine suggests that Angad Bedi has been approached for the role.

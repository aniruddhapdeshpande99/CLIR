Javed Akhtar, Sameer Deny Association With Modi Biopic, Producer Says He's Used Their Old Songs

Authors : Garima Satija,

Published on : 23/03/2019, 15:53:08

Yesterday, Javed Akhtar slammed the filmmakers clarifying that despite the fact his name appears in the credits, he has not written any song for PM Narendra Modi's biopic. And today, lyricist Sameer also raised his voice to deny his association with the film as well.

“Am shocked to find my name on the poster of this film. Have not written any songs for it!” – when Javed Akhtar tweeted this yesterday, it stirred up a storm on Twitter. guiltfree.online Since the time Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s biopic has been announced, it is making headlines for some or the other reason. First, it was because of the trolls on social media after the poster starring Vivek Oberoi was released, and then when the trailer was out, it yet again became the butt of jokes. I haven't written any song for the movie. I don't understand what they are crediting me for,” he was quoted as saying by Mid-day. Twitter On the other hand, producer of the film Sandip Ssingh has clarified that the makers have used their old songs in the movie. “We have taken the songs 'Ishwar Allah' from the film '1947:Earth' and the song 'Suno Gaur Se Duniya Walon' from the film 'Dus' in our film, thus we have given the due credits to respective lyricists Javed Sahab and Sameer Ji. @TSeries is our Music partner,” he wrote on Twitter.

AR Rahman's Marvel Anthem For Avengers: Endgame Is Out And Fans Are Disappointed AR Rahman's Marvel Anthem For Avengers: Endgame Is Out And Fans Are Disappointed Just a few days ago, Indian film critic and trade analyst Taran Adarsh had confirmed that Rahman will be composing a song for Indian fans in three languages, Hindi, Tamil, and Telugu. Finally, after the short and sweet wait, the Marvel Anthem in Hindi composed by music maestro AR Rahman is finally out. To share the news with the world, Marvel India tweeted the video and wrote: Screengrab "Here's something special for all you amazing Marvel India fans!" The anthem by AR Rahman was at an event in Mumbai. Rahman's anthem is as perfect as it could get as it perfectly sums up the Avengers' resolution to take down Thanos - "Whatever it takes." In the second half of the song, you get to go on a ride that includes Captain America, Iron Man, Hawkeye, Thor, and Black Widow.

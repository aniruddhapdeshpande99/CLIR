"Begum, ishq ka mazaa hi chupke chupke se karne mein hai..." Even if you're a 90's kid, this line will ring a bell and teleport you into the good old days of Hindi Cinema when love stories were simple and heartwarming. Twitter Hrishikesh Mukherjee’s iconic comedy Chupke Chupke, starring Dharmendra, Amitabh Bachchan, Sharmila Tagore and Jaya Bachchan in lead roles is a film that is still remembered by everyone. Be it their performances or the dialogues, the film turned out to be a cult hit. The film also completed 44 years of release on 11th April this year. And now, the film is getting rebooted for a solid remake which will be backed by producers Bhushan Kumar and Luv Ranjan. According to a Mumbai Mirror report, Rajkummar Rao is in talks with the production house to reprise Dharmendra's role in the film.

2013 musical Begin Again featuring Keira Knightley and Mark Ruffalo is getting a Desi spin as Bollywood is all set to make an official remake. Just to jog your memory a little, the story revolved around the life of a singer-songwriter who is discovered by a struggling record label executive and collaborates with him to produce an album recorded in public locations all over New York City. Bhushan Kumar’s T-Series and Vikram Malhotra-led Abundantia Entertainment are teaming up to develop the Hindi adaptation of Hollywood film Begin Again.

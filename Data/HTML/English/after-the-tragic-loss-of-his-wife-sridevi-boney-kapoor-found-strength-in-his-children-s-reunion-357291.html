There are some losses in life that never let you recover fully. Losing a loved one to death is certainly one of them. For Boney Kapoor and his family, life turned upside down with the tragic loss of Sridevi. Agencies The veteran actress passed away in Dubai after the accidental drowning in the hotel bath-tub. The entire Kapoor clan came together to mourn this loss. This sudden loss extremely terrible for the entire family, especially her kids. Till today, neither the kids nor her husband Boney Kapoor has recovered fully from it. Viral Bhayani In fact, we have seen him tear up and break down at several public events. Recently, the filmmaker was in Goa with daughter Janhvi Kapoor, where he again mentioned his wife and spoke about the loss that still feels heavy in the heart. The only consolation that became the medicine to his grief was seeing all his children uniting. This tragedy brought all his children including Arjun Kapoor, Janhvi Kapoor, and Khushi Kapoor together and that became his strength to overcome it all. He added, Agencies "The children coming together gave me a lot of strength to cope up with the situation we were facing. I couldn’t have asked for more. For a parent, children come first and seeing all my 4 children together was….kind of a balming effect after the tragedy."

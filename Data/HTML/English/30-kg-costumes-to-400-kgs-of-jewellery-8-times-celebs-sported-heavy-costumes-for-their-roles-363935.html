In Bollywood, weight is something that really matters. No, we are not talking about Bollywood divas' obsession with size zero. We are talking about the weight in the characters that they play, or the weight in the costumes that they don for their roles. From Madhuri Dixit's eye-catching purple saree in Hum Aapke Hai Koun to Deepika Padukone’s extravagant lehengas in Bajirao Mastani, do you know that some of these costumes are crazily heavy? When the fire broke down on the sets of Sanjay Leela Bhansali’s Padmaavat, a lot of costumes along with the sets were burned. For a layman like us, that wasn’t a big damage, right? But those who have watched Sanjay Leela Bhansali’s movies would know about his penchant for larger than life sets and costumes. That was a huge loss for him.

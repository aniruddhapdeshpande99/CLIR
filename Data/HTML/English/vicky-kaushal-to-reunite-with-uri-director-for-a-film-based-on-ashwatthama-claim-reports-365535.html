According to a Mumbai Mirror report, Vicky and Aditya are now on-board for a period-war drama based on the mythological character of Ashwathama from the Mahabharata. The film will be produced by Ronnie Screwvala, who had also produced Uri. For the upcoming untitled film based on Ashwatthama, the son of Dronacharya, Vicky Kaushal be seen in the titular role. The film will be shot in multiple locations across the country and will be going on the floors later this year and the team is targeting for a mid-year release in 2020. The report quotes a source close to the project saying, Agencies “The film will be mounted on a big scale and will be shot later this year. Aditya is already neck-deep in research and will go on a recce soon.

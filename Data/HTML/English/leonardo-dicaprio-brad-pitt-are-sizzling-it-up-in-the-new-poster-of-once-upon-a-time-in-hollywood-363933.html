Hollywood actor Brad Pitt has joined Leonardo DiCaprio in for an upcoming Manson movie. The film is titled 'Once Upon A Time in Hollywood'. Twitter Tarantino, who is writing and directing the film, has described the project as “a story that takes place in Los Angeles in 1969, at the height of hippy Hollywood." And the new posters are giving the same vibe. Take a look. Twitter DiCaprio will play Rick Dalton, former star of a western TV series, while Pitt will be his longtime stunt double, Cliff Booth. “Both are struggling to make it in a Hollywood they don’t recognise anymore. But Rick has a very famous next-door neighbour...Sharon Tate,” said Tarantino in an interview.

Their twitter chat is something everyone will relate to. Big B complained on twitter about keeping mum on Badla success to the producer of the film, Shah Rukh Khan. He mentioned how big Badla has become but there is not even a single appreciation post about the success. He wrote, “. About time someone started talking about this silent success .. !! because neither the Producer, nor the distributor, nor the on line Producer, or any else in the Industry, in general .. has even spent a nano second to compliment the success of this film #Badla .. thk u”. But as usual SRK had the wittiest reply on it, he wrote, “Sir hum toh wait kar rahein hain ki aap party kab de rahein hain hum sabko! We r waiting outside Jalsa every nite! “.

Anurag Kashyap Extends Support To Actor Savi Sidhu, Who Now Works As A Guard To Make Ends Meet

Authors : Garima Satija,

Published on : 20/03/2019, 11:34:39

Savi Sidhu’s story went viral on social media. It was soon noticed by filmmaker Anurag Kashyap who has worked with him in his films. Reading the heartwrenching story of Savi Sidhu, Anurag Kashyap poured his heart out narrating how he was once a waiter too. He also said that he respects Savi Sidhu for leading taking up a job and not begging.

It was both shocking and heartbreaking to come to terms with the story of actor Savi Sidhu who is struggling to support his family. The man who has played prominent roles in movies like Black Friday, Gulaal and Pataila House had no film offers and was finding it tough to support his family financially. And so, he chose to take up the job of a security guard in order to the make ends meet. Now, Savi Sidhu works 12 hours a day in a housing society. He was forced to take up this job as he had no money. The actor whose films were once played on the big screen had no money to now go and watch a film in the theatre.

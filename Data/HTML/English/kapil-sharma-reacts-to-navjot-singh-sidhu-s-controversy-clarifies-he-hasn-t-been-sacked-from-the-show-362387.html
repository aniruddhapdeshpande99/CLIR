Kapil Sharma Reacts To Navjot Singh Sidhu's Controversy, Clarifies He Hasn’t Been Sacked From The Show

Authors : Garima Satija,

Published on : 19/02/2019, 11:31:03

It was reported that Navjot Singh Sidhu has been sacked for the Kapil Sharma Show. Finally, the comedian has broken silence on the entire hoopla. In a video, which was shared by Navjot Singh Sidhu, Kapil can be seen clarifying the reports stating that he has been ousted from the show.

The Pulwama terror attack was so deadly that not only it killed over 44 CRPF soldiers but also has left the entire nation fighting among our own-selves. After Navjot Singh Sidhu’s controversial remark on the terrorist attack, hashtags #BoycottSidhu, #BoycottKapilSharmaShow and #BoycottSonyTV were trending on Twitter. “For a handful of people, can you blame the entire nation and can you blame an individual?,” Sidhu had said earlier adding, “It (the attack) is a cowardly act and I condemn it firmly. Violence is always condemnable and those who did it must be punished.” It was reported that Navjot Singh Sidhu has been sacked for the Kapil Sharma Show. Finally, the comedian Kapil himself has broken silence on the entire hoopla. In a video, which was shared by Navjot Singh Sidhu, Kapil can be seen clarifying the reports stating that he has not been ousted from the show.

Kangana Ranaut has had issues with Alia Bhatt and it isn't new to any of us. Not sure how and when the animosity brew but Kangana has been targeting Bhatt since a long time now. A few months ago, she had alleged that the Raazi star didn’t reciprocate her gesture and help promote Manikarnika: The Queen of Jhansi. She went on to calling Alia a puppet of producer Karan Johar and urged her to ‘to grow a spine’. And just few days ago, in an interview to Bollywood Life, Kangana had said that she finds comparisons between herself and Alia embarrassing because she found Alia’s critically acclaimed performance in Gully Boy ‘mediocre’. In a rather graceful response to Kangana's attack, Alia spoke to Bollywood life and said, Agencies “I respect Kangana’s work a lot and I respect her opinion. If she feels a particular way she must have reason to feel that way. I would rather remember how much she had praised me after watching Raazi. And I’d just like to focus on my work. Maybe she will appreciate me again if I work hard enough.” No matter what Kangana says about Alia, her response proves that she will never stoop low and maintain poise and grace.

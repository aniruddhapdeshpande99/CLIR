Adah Sharma is one actress who proved her mettle with her first film 1920. But soon after 1920, we didn’t see her much, obviously because she had no strong contacts or a KJo debut. Despite that, Adah Sharma is happy with her life and is infact excited about her film Moh, where she plays a girl who is facing depression. Twitter An excited Adah, who was also seen in Hasee Toh Phasee, talks to Indiatimes about her film Moh and her journey in Bollywood so far. Talking about Moh, she said, “When I was narrated the script, I was attached with the emotional journey, I thought its a fantastic emotional film for me to do, and equally challenging, I play this honest girl who is in depression and it shows how she got into depression. We show the reason because she was a happy girl who didn’t care about what people used to tell about her, I think people are going to really like it.” Don't Miss 12.5 K SHARES 5.5 K SHARES 3.4 K SHARES 3.1 K SHARES 2.5 K SHARES Twitter We quizzed her if she ever faced depression in real life and she said, “Personally, I haven’t faced depression. Of course, I have had my low moments on days, because you can’t be happy 24/7, you want to be sad also, but I haven’t felt depression” Twitter Talking about how one can heal themselves from depression and Adah has some valid points after her movie, She said, “I can say that those who are depressed, should outdo themselves from that situation and help others maybe. Visiting an old age home or helping animals may help them a lot. Of course you do need medical attention but one thing is important that when you are sad, low or depressed, don’t think about yourself at that time and try and think outer of yourself and do something for somebody else.” Twitter We asked Adah if she ever gets insecure that other actresses are doing so well and getting powerful roles, and she replies, “It would be very wrong for me to say that someone is someone’s daughter and that is why she got this or that role. I can crib about that or I can say that I actually started with a fantastic role and got appreciation for my first film which is 1920. After all, somebody who didn’t know me at all trusted me and gave the film." "I have done some great work down South also. I have Commando 2 which is one of the biggest action film franchise in our country. I think I am very lucky, instead of thinking that a particular role didn’t come to me because she is some else's child. I am very grateful for what I got.” When asked if she fears losing what she's achieved in such a short span of time and she has the most zen answer, “ Not at all, because I don’t think I have anything to lose right now. At the moment, I am getting so much love, like recently, I wore a newspaper dress on a red carpet, I wore it because I liked it, and it was so heartening to see that people loved it too and thought it was cool. Basically, I don’t do things to get love, I do it what I like." Further continuing, she said, “Talent you can never lose, no one can take that away from you, thankfully. It’s only yours, and talking about love from people, I feel I will get it if I deserve it ." Twitter Adah is extremely confident and positive, in fact she wants good directors to come to her rather than she having to ask for work, “ I am hoping filmmakers watch my films and think lets have her in our film, let’s give her more opportunities, because she is talented and she can do everything. I want that to happen, I don’t have small dreams of being launched by this person, , I want others to say wow, let’s take Adah, because it’s so nice to be on the set when somebody thinks you are really good.” Undoubtedly, Adah is one focussed actress and she's definitely here to stay and slay!

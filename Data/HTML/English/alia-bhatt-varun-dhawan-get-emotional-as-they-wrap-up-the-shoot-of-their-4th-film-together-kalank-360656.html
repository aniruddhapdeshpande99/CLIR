Authors : Garima Satija,

Alia Bhatt and Varun Dhawan had made their Bollywood debut with Dharma Productions’ Student Of The Year in 2012. After having done three films together – that include Student Of The Year, Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania, and Badrinath Ki Dulhania, they are all set to star in yet another project titled Kalank.

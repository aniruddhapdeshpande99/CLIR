Their fate and shelf live majorly depend on how their films fare at the box-office. When it comes to box-office success, Aamir Khan is one of the most bankable actors who has given some solid blockbusters in the last few years. However, Thugs Of Hindostan was an exception that his fans would want to forget and erase from their memory. The multi-starrer film starring Amitabh Bachchan, Katrina Kaif and Fatima Sana Shaikh was one of the biggest disasters of 2018. Instead of dodging the questions around the failure of the film, Aamir has always taken it in his stride and bared his heart honestly. After the release, he also extended his sincere apology for the failure. Talking to media, he had said, “I would like to take full responsibility for that.

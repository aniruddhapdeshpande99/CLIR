Swara Bhasker, who had stirred up a storm on social media because of her masturbation scene in Veere Di Wedding, launched upcoming TV show Courtroom – a show inspired by real life court cases. The show will focus unveil proceedings from charges to verdicts of cases that had successful judgements that retained the faith of people in judiciary. BCCL Swara Bhasker was asked about social media and its trials and how it has become a court itself. To this, the actress said that social media is meant for connecting people and not for mob trails. “I am actually against this idea of Twitter justice. These days the trial and the justice both happen on Twitter itself. I think social media is a platform for connecting but you can’t replace our judiciary with mob trials on social media. That’s such a dangerous thing to ask for,” she was quoted as saying by Indian Express.

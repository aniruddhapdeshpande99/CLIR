Recently, speaking about the #MeToo movement that was kickstarted in the country last year, she opened up about the progress and aftermath of the movement. Twitter Admitting that there has been a positive change in the industry, Kalki Koechlin said that there’s a lot of work to be done. When she was asked if she has ever faced harassment in the industry, the Gully Boy actress said that her stance on feminism is quite solid and she hasn’t faced such a situation. “My stance on feminism is quite solid and transparent. I have a big Fuck off sign on my forehead, so no I haven’t faced it. But what I do see is that there is a lot of casual sexism. That happens when someone makes fun of your body. I’ve experienced that. But I’ve been very clear about my stand from the start, so I haven’t had anything dramatic happen to me,” she was quoted as saying by India Today.

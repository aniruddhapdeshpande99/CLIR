Here’s An Update On Sacred Games S2! Kalki Koechlin Reportedly Joins The Cast & We're Elated

Authors : Garima Satija,

Published on : 30/03/2019, 18:11:25

Fans have been waiting for Sacred Games season 2. Now, a report in Bollywood Hungama suggests that Kalki Koechlin, known for her roles in Anurag Kashyap’s Dev D and recently released Netflix show Made in Heaven, has been roped in for season 2. It is said that she will be playing a pivotal role.

The first episode of the Sacred Games started with a bang and we knew it is not like any other regular Indian shows. When the first season had aired, it was so intriguing that almost everyone had binged watched it. The popularity of the Netflix India’s original show was so huge that Nawazuddin Siddiqui once said that people would recognise him as Ganesh Gaitonde in Italy. Fans have been waiting for season 2 now to know why and how the city will be decimated in 25 days. Nawazuddin Siddiqui had earlier teased that season 2 will be the baap of the first one. “Sab Marenge, sirf Trivedi Bachega”. But why and how, everyone is still wondering. Now, a report in Bollywood Hungama suggests that Kalki Koechlin, known for her roles in Anurag Kashyap’s Dev D and recently released Amazon Prime's show Made in Heaven, has been roped in for season 2. It is said that she will be playing a pivotal role. Some time ago, Netflix had teased that “14 din mein kuch bada hone wala hai”. And it raised the excitement level of the fans. Calendar nikaal. Sacred Games will be back for Season 2. pic.twitter.com/lSBIzQR2b9 — Netflix India (@NetflixIndia) September 21, 2018 Meanwhile, Pankaj Tripathi who played a small role of the mysterious Guruji in the first season of much-hyped Netflix series Sacred Games, had earlier hinted that he will play a pivotal role in season 2. “I don’t know if my character will be a main one in the next season. I shot for the series for just one day after the main shooting was over. They thought ‘Let’s bring this character into the light’. Initially, that was not the plan. Something happened that they didn’t announce this differently and I am not someone who likes tom-tomming. It was a small role in the beginning, so we did not promote it as if it were a main one in the first season,” he had said.

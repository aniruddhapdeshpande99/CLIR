Twitter Indiatimes met this inspiring soul to have a chat with him about his new venture in music, where we spoke to him about films, success, life and everything in between. However, I always wanted to be an actor first and then a singer”. Twitter Right now Ayushmann is the most talked about actor in the industry especially with the kind of films he is doing, we informed him how even Ranveer Singh feels why he isn’t getting the kind of films Ayushmann is doing, to which the actor said, “ It’s very kind of him to say that, but genuinely what I like, I do. I think success also depends on the people, how audience grasps your script, and how much they resonate with it, so I owe my success to the audience at the same time.” Twitter We asked him to reveal the secret mantra on choosing the right scripts, he said, “Most of my decisions are impulsive, you must be thinking I think a lot before taking a decision but its impulsive, because your first reaction is the most organic reaction, be it first reaction to a script, song or any particular talent which I will see on this platform. I have a very intuitive reaction, that’s about it. It’s as simple as that, I think as an audience, I don’t think like a star, jaise audience grasp karti hai kisi concept ko, mai waise hi karta hu.” Twitter The moment Ayushmann said he doesn’t think like a star, we asked him if he has started feeling like a star, he said, “Since you are giving successful films you think that you have become a star but you should not take it to your head that’s the most important thing. If you have that connection with the audience, you have to be the audience, aapko unki tarah hi rehna padega and I have no other option but to do that because the kind of films that I am doing a middle of the road cinema and they deal with realism, so mujhe real hi rehna padega , that’s my zone.” Twitter The man who has given back-to-back hits still craves to do a lot more in his life, “I want to do everything, I want to produce, direct, compose more act more, have more beautiful scripts coming my way. I am very restless as an artist so I just crave to do every single thing which satiates my soul.” Before ending our interview we asked him what’s his biggest fear, he said, “I am very fearless, kyuki jis tarike ka risk mai leta lu cinema mai bhi aur apne life ke saath, so I feel I am quite happy go, lucky person”. We couldn’t agree more. His vibe is genuinely so enigmatic that you just cannot get enough of him.

Bollywood actor Vivek Oberoi was in news recently for starring in the biopic of Prime Minister Narendra Modi. Receiving severe backlash on the subject and the timing of its release (11th April), Vivek Oberoi took on criticism and gave a befitting reply. Twitter From being seen on debates to heavily promoting his upcoming film, speaking about his off and on stint in Bollywood, Vivek Oberoi said, "I'm still here and unbreakable." His film was earlier touted to release on 4th April, however, opposition parties alleged that the movie has an intention of influencing voters in the run-up to the Lok Sabha elections. Twitter Soon the movie shifted to 11th April but now in a fresh set of events, EC has banned the biopic citing 'disturbs level-playing field' They have mentioned there will be no biopic till the polls end.

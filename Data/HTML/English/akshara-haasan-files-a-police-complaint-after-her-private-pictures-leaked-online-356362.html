Akshara Haasan, Kamal Haasan’s daughter was in headlines due to her private pictures being leaked online. Her bikini pictures were leaked online and it went viral in a very short span of time. Akshara who has been the victim of cybercrime, immediately went to the police station and filed a complaint against it.

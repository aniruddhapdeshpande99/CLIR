Taapsee Pannu Thinks She Isn’t ‘Extraordinarily Good Looking,' Calls It Her Biggest Strength

Authors : Garima Satija,

Published on : 09/03/2019, 16:49:21

Taapsee Pannu thinks her biggest strength in the industry is her average looks which makers her resonate with average Indian women. “I am an average Indian girl, who isn’t extraordinarily good-looking and that is my biggest strength,” she was quoted as saying by TOI.

Unlike other actresses, Taapsee Pannu wasn’t ever inclined towards doing glamorous roles. She chose performance oriented roles and proved her mettle with every film. From Pink to Naam Shabana and Badla recently, the diva has come a long way. She is considered one of the finest actresses in the industry today. With no godfather and connections in the industry, her journey in Bollywood wasn’t a cake walk. She was often replaced and rejected too.

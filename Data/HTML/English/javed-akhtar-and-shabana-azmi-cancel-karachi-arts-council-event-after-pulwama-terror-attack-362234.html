Javed Akhtar And Shabana Azmi Cancel Karachi Arts Council Event After Pulwama Terror Attack

Authors : Garima Satija,

Published on : 15/02/2019, 18:25:26

Following Pulwama Terror Attack, Javed Akhtar and Shabana Azmi have decided to not attend Kaifi Azmi's birth centenary celebrations in Karachi. The couple was invited to Pakistan by the Karachi Arts Council for a two-day event. She announced the decision on Twitter.

The entire nation is grieving. People are angry and heartbroken at the same time. At least 44 Jawans of Central Reserve Police Force (CPRF) were killed in the deadly terror attack. Suicide bomber Adil Ahmad Dar from terrorist organisation Jaish-e-Mohammad rammed rammed his SUV laden with 300 kg of explosives into the bus, which was a 70-vehicle convoy, of jawans.

Vishal Bhardwaj Points Out Error In National Anthem, Tells I&B Ministry To Get It Rectified

Authors : Garima Satija,

Published on : 29/10/2018, 17:46:45

Vishal Bhardwaj who attended the festival noticed an error in the national anthem that was played there. He said that there was a technical glitch in the National Anthem produced by Films Division and Ministry of Information & Broadcasting. Complaining about the same and urging I&B ministry to rectify it, here’s what he posted on Twitter.

Earlier this year, Supreme Court accepted that government’s suggestion to play National Anthem before a film in a movie theatre isn’t mandatory. However, a lot of cinema halls still play it before the screening of movies. That is what happened at the MAMI film festival as well.

A Movie On 26/11 Martyr Major Sandeep Unnikrishnan Is On The Cards, Mahesh Babu To Produce It A Movie On 26/11 Martyr Major Sandeep Unnikrishnan Is On The Cards, Mahesh Babu To Produce It Popular Telugu actor Mahesh Babu is foraying into production with the film titled Major. The movie will be based on based on the life of Major Sandeep Unnikrishnan, a National Security Guards (NSG) commando who lost his life while saving several hostages from the deadly 26/11 attack. The movie will be released in 2020. Prominent young actor Adivi Sesh will play the lead. Sesh has also written the film, which will be directed by Goodachari director Shashikiran Tikka. The film is co-produced by Adivi Entertainment and Sharath Chandra and Anurag Reddy's A+S Movies. The production and distribution studio in India and Telugu superstar Mahesh Babu's production house G Mahesh Babu Entertainment (GMB) have closed a deal to produce Major - a Hindi and Telugu bi-lingual film, read a statement. The deal marks Sony Pictures' entry into the Telugu film industry as it expands its regional footprint, as well as GMB's entry into Hindi cinema.

The ace rapper and singer Badshah is now planning to make his Bollywood debut, in fact, he confessed to Indiatimes that he is eager to become a hero. Badshah, who released his new song ‘She Move it Like’ is looking forward to enter the acting arena and become a lead actor. Instagram Indiatimes had a candid conversation with the singer, where we quizzed him if he wants to work in films to which he happily replied, “Of course, I am doing a film, I was offered two films, one I didn’t do and the other one I lost to Diljit Paaji, because he is a better actor. That film was Good News with Akshay Kumar. I am reading scripts and one of it is finalized also, I have a lot of desire to become a hero, do brand endorsements, I want to experience what being a hero feels like.” Instagram We also asked the singer if he fears losing his success, his reply won our hearts, "I don’t take this star-tag very seriously. I have just become impatient after achieving success, otherwise, I am still the same. I was patient earlier but now I am not. I have a lot of ambitious, I want to do a lot of things, I am hungry for good work and I feel I have very less time to do so much and things aren’t happening the way i want them to. But I don’t have that air that I am star, sometimes I feel that I should feel like a star and throw tantrums but I genuinely can’t do that.

Twitter Currently, Nawazuddin Siddiqui is one of the most sought after and versatile actors in the B-town. From playing a gangster in Sacred Games to doing Nandita Das' Manto, he has proved his mettle as an actor with every film. In his next, he will be seen essaying the role of Bal Thackeray. Twitter For the promotions of the movie, he recently appeared on The Kapil Sharma Show along with film’s leading lady Amrita Rao and shared some interesting anecdotes from his struggling days. Talking about the time when he was a junior artist for an ad, he revealed he was paid a sum of Rs 4000.

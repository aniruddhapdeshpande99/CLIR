'Avengers: Endgame' Fever Grips Mumbai! Celebrities Attend The Screening Of The Film!

Authors : Isha Sharma,

Published on : 27/04/2019, 12:05:24

Even celebrities in Mumbai are bit by the Marvel bug as many celebrities were spotted attending the screening of the film in Mumbai last evening. While the film is already off to a good-start in India, the turn out of celebrities in the theatres just proved that they are big Marvel fans too!

Just like each one of us, who was eagerly waiting for Avengers Endgame to release, Bollywood celebrities were waiting with a baited breath too! "The things we do for love," is how a Marvel fan reacted when he managed to book a 1.40 am the show for Avengers: Endgame. It seems odd to quote Jaime Lannister from Game of Thrones in the "Avengers" context but such is the frenzy that fans will watch any show they can over the weekend -- whether at 1.40 am or at 6 am -- even if it means spending big bucks. And still, no tickets are available. To accommodate the demand for the last of the Avenger series, which released on Friday, some theatres are running shows 24X7.

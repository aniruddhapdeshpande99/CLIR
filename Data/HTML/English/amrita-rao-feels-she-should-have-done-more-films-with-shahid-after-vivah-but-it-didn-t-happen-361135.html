Her claim to fame was Ishq Visq and Main Hoon Na and she soon became a popular face. Families connected with her innocence and choice of films. But she soon took a gap after her wedding. And now the actress is back again with Thackeray. She will be seen playing Meenatal alongside Nawazuddin Siddiqui. In of our earlier interviews, Amrita had revealed how she lost out on big projects because she has no sugar daddies. She also has reservations in doing love making scenes. She said: "As the cinema is changing, on-screen kissing, love-making scenes are becoming part of the narrative. I am not saying it is wrong, because it is the reflection of how our society has changed and become comfortable with it. But I am uncomfortable performing it on screen. Love-making is so personal to me that if I do it on screen, it is like I am leaving a part of my soul. I cannot do that."

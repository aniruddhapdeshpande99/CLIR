She has time and again spoken about her son Taimur in her interview. The social media superstar that her son is, she again spoke to Rajeev Masand and opened up about her son Taimur and in a first, she called him a terror, very naughty and exactly like his father (Saif Ali Khan). Sharing a detail of how it is to see Taimur grow up, Kareena said, “Now he is going through that phase where he says, ‘Mama, don’t laugh’, ‘Mama don’t cough’, everything we are doing and he says ‘Mama, don’t do that’. I think it’s because we are always like ‘Taimur, don’t do that’ so he is now picking it and telling us.” She added that the constant media attention does bother her. She added, “He is so innocent that since he has been told that ‘you have to say hi and hello to everyone’, he is like ‘hi’ while waving to the media. They (paparazzi) say hello, they are very polite and are always at a distance. But they do constant kind of movement. The only thing I can give him is for him to go out and play, walk the street, like we do." However, she insisted that she is working hard as a mother to make sure that her son lives a normal life.
